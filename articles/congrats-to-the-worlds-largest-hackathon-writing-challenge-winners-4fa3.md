# Congrats to the World's Largest Hackathon Writing Challenge Winners!
**Author:** Jess Lee
**Published:** August 21, 2025

## Overview
Winners of the World's Largest Hackathon Writing Challenge celebrating stories from builders of all experience levels.

## Key Concepts

### Grand Prize Winners
1. **After the Hack:** Laurel Beyers - Hackathon participation reigniting creative confidence during career transition
2. **Beyond the Code:** Yan Leong - Journey from coding novice to shipping full-stack AI app with OpenAI voice dictation and Stripe payments
3. **Building with Bolt:** Jake Dibattista - Learn2Play combining RAG models, video agents, and marketplace functionality

Seven additional projects received recognition for creativity, team dynamics, solo development, and mentorship.
