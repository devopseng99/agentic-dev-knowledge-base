# What is Agentic AI? A Complete Guide to Autonomous AI Systems
**Author:** Jayaveer Bhupalam  **Published:** November 27, 2025

## Overview
Introduces the concept of Agentic AI as autonomous systems that can independently make decisions and take actions. Positions Agentic AI as distinct from traditional AI by emphasizing autonomy with governance.

## Key Concepts

- Non-Agentic AI vs. Agentic AI distinctions
- What Makes AI "Agentic": goal-setting, planning, tool use, memory, and self-correction
- Real-world applications and examples
- The principle of autonomy paired with oversight
- Getting started with Agentic AI implementation
- Evolution from assistant-based AI to action-oriented AI

**Core Properties of Agentic AI**
1. Perceive context from multiple inputs
2. Plan and reason about how to achieve a goal
3. Execute actions via connected tools, APIs, or workflows
4. Learn from feedback and adjust behavior
5. Collaborate with other agents in multi-agent networks
