# DeFAI is Coming: How AI Agents Will Reshape DeFi (And Why They Need Privacy)
**Author:** sid
**Published:** August 28, 2025

## Overview
Argues that if your AI trading bot's strategy is visible on-chain, it is a sitting duck for MEV bots. Presents confidential AI agents using Trusted Execution Environments as the solution.

## Key Concepts

### The Visibility Problem
When AI trading logic executes on public blockchains, MEV bots study behavior patterns, execute front-running attacks, perform sandwich attacks on large orders, and target liquidation mechanisms.

### Why MEV Bots Exploit Predictable Agents
AI agents operate through recognizable patterns -- purchasing at specific thresholds, swapping at consistent intervals, applying uniform risk protocols. MEV bots specialize in pattern recognition.

### Confidential AI Agents
- Maintain strategy logic within TEEs
- Execute transactions privately without revealing intent
- Preserve competitive advantages through encrypted decision-making
- Deliver verifiable outcomes without methodology disclosure

### Real-World Implementations
- **Tradable**: ROFL-based platform with encrypted portfolio analysis in TEEs
- **Flashback Labs**: Federated learning through ROFL, training models on encrypted datasets
- **Plurality Network**: Confidential reputation systems aggregating social information privately

### Key Distinction
"Verifiable doesn't mean visible." Deploy AI logic in hardware-protected enclaves, process confidential data without exposing it, generate cryptographic execution proofs, publish verified outcomes on-chain.
