# MindCare AI: Revolutionizing Mental Health Support with Compassionate AI
**Author:** ANIRUDDHA ADAK  **Published:** July 11, 2025

## Overview
World's Largest Hackathon Writing Challenge submission describing MindCare AI, a web-based mental health support platform combining AI with human connection. Addresses accessibility gaps in mental health care by offering round-the-clock support powered by Google's Gemini AI alongside volunteer networks.

## Key Concepts
- AI-Powered Assessment: Gemini API analyzes user input for mental health risk evaluation
- 24/7 Chatbot Support: empathetic conversational AI for stress, anxiety, and depression management
- Personalized Tools: breathing exercises and journaling prompts tailored to emotional states
- Human Connection: volunteer network provides supplementary human support
- Privacy Architecture: enterprise-grade encryption with HIPAA-aligned compliance
- Ethical Boundaries: explicit safeguards preventing AI from replacing professional medical care
- Tech Stack: React + TypeScript, TailwindCSS, Framer Motion, Node.js + Python, Flask, Google Gemini API
- Live: https://mindcareai.netlify.app
