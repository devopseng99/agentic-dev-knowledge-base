# 7 Popular AI Video Generation Tools
**Author:** Serenepine  **Published:** October 20, 2024

## Overview
The article surveys rapid advancements in AI video generation, highlighting seven prominent tools transforming content creation across industries including marketing, education, and filmmaking.

## Key Concepts
- Text-to-video and image-to-video conversion
- AI virtual presenters and avatars
- Automated video editing and enhancement
- Long-form to short-form video transformation
- Multi-language support capabilities

## Tools Covered
1. **Runway** (text/image-to-video, editing, green screen)
2. **Pika** (text-to-video, simplicity-focused)
3. **Synthesia** (AI presenters, multi-language)
4. **Fliki** (text-to-video with music/narration)
5. **Lumen5** (social media video creation)
6. **Pictory** (long-to-short video conversion)
7. **DeepBrain AI** (realistic AI presenters)
