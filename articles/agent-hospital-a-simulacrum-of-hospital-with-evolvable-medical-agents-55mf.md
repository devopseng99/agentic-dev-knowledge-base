# Agent Hospital: A Simulacrum of Hospital with Evolvable Medical Agents
**Author:** aimodels-fyi  **Published:** May 8, 2024 (Edited January 18, 2026)

## Overview
Summarizes a research paper introducing "Agent Hospital," a virtual hospital environment designed to develop and test AI-powered medical agents. The framework enables agents to learn and improve their clinical decision-making capabilities through simulated interactions with patients and healthcare scenarios.

## Key Concepts
- Evolvable Medical Agents: AI systems that adapt and enhance performance through reinforcement learning within the simulated environment
- Virtual Hospital Testbed: realistic simulation including departments, staff roles, and patient cases for controlled agent evaluation
- Large Language Model Integration: LLMs simulate authentic patient interactions and conversations
- Automated Scenario Generation: techniques to create high-quality medical simulation scenarios for enriched training
- Clinical Decision Support: development pathway toward AI systems assisting real-world healthcare providers
