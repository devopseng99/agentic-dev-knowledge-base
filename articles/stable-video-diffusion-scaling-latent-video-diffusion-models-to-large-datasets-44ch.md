# Stable Video Diffusion: Scaling Latent Video Diffusion Models to Large Datasets
**Author:** Paperium  **Published:** December 25, 2025

## Overview
This article discusses a technical advancement in video generation technology. The work focuses on scaling diffusion models specifically designed to operate in latent space rather than pixel space, enabling more efficient training on large-scale video datasets.

## Key Concepts

- **Latent Video Diffusion:** Operating diffusion processes in compressed latent representations rather than raw pixel data
- **Scalability:** Methods for training video generation models on significantly larger datasets
- **Efficiency:** Reduced computational requirements compared to pixel-space approaches
- **Video Generation:** Synthesis of realistic video content from various input modalities

Stable Video Diffusion lets you make short clips from simple text or a single photo, using large curated videos to help the model learn realistic motion.
