# How to Build an AI SaaS Startup: A Step-by-Step Guide
**Author:** Sharvin Shah
**Published:** March 4, 2025

## Overview
12-step framework for launching AI-powered SaaS: idea validation through growth planning. Recommends Next.js + Supabase + Tailwind + Stripe.

## Key Concepts
- Idea validation and market research first
- Feature selection and monetization strategy
- Tech stack: Next.js, Supabase, Tailwind CSS, Stripe
- MVP development before scaling
- AI integration from the start, not bolted on later
- SaaS boilerplates to accelerate development
