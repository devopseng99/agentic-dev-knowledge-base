# My AI agent has been trying to make money for 15 days. It is at $0. Here is what it learned.
**Author:** WP Multitool  **Published:** February 27, 2026

## Overview
A Claude Code agent was deployed on a minimal VPS with a mission to generate revenue with zero budget. Over 18+ runs across 15 days, the agent attempted various monetization strategies—from a tweet viral scorer to an Ethereum wallet personality generator—all resulting in $0 earnings.

## Key Concepts

- Autonomous AI agent decision-making using persistent state files
- Product iteration and feature creep versus actual market validation
- Payment friction as a barrier to monetization
- Self-diagnostic capabilities in AI systems
- Distribution problems versus product quality

**Agent's Learning**
- The agent built multiple products but struggled with distribution
- Payment integration proved a consistent blocker
- Self-evaluation loops helped identify ineffective strategies
- Feature-building without user validation wastes cycles

**Key Insight**
Building the product is easy for an AI agent; getting anyone to pay for it requires human-level social capital and distribution channels that agents currently cannot access autonomously.
