# Why Modern Manufacturers Are Choosing ERPNext Services and Custom ERP Software for Operational Excellence
**Author:** Sigzen Technologies  **Published:** June 23, 2025

## Overview
Modern manufacturers face pressure to increase productivity and reduce costs. Many rely on fragmented systems and outdated software that hinders growth. ERPNext and custom ERP software provide the solution.

## Key Concepts

**Why Manufacturing ERP Is Essential**
Without integrated systems, manufacturers face:
- Inventory inaccuracies
- Production delays
- Inconsistent reporting
- Poor customer satisfaction
- Inefficient resource utilization

**Benefits of ERPNext Services**
1. End-to-End Implementation — complete deployment tailored to workflows
2. Custom Module Development — extensions for dashboards, workflows, and machine integration
3. Training and Support — team adaptation and self-reliance building
4. Upgrades and Maintenance — regular updates and security management

**Key ERPNext Manufacturing Features**
- Bill of Materials (BOM) hierarchy
- Production Orders and scheduling
- Workstations and routing assignment
- Real-time inventory control
- Automated quality inspection
- Subcontracting management
- Multi-level and project-based manufacturing

**Real-World Case Study**
Client: Mid-sized auto component manufacturer
Challenges: Manual inventory, inconsistent production data, high material waste
Outcomes:
- 40% reduction in production downtime
- 99% inventory accuracy achieved
- Real-time process visibility
- Implementation completed in 12 weeks
