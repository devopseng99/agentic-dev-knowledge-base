# Telemedicine capabilities expanded through artificial intelligence
**Author:** Abto Software  **Published:** December 13, 2023 (Edited July 18, 2025)

## Overview
Examines how artificial intelligence enhances telemedicine platforms. AI transforms healthcare delivery by automating processes, improving diagnostics, and enabling remote care. Explores specific AI applications and addresses implementation challenges in telemedicine contexts.

## Key Concepts
- Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM): AI systems track health indicators in real-time, enabling automated analysis and personalized recommendations
- Natural Language Processing (NLP): AI transcribes patient information, maintains documentation, performs sentiment analysis and inquiry classification
- Medical Imaging: deep learning detects abnormal patterns like tumors and fractures through automated image analysis
- Decision Support: machine learning generates treatment recommendations and alerts regarding drug interactions
- Virtual Assistants/Chatbots: AI handles patient triage, appointment scheduling, and information retrieval
- Pose Detection: AI analyzes body positioning for physical therapy guidance and progress tracking
- Security & Ethics: data protection and maintaining human oversight in clinical decision-making
