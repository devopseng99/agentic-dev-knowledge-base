# AI Agents Are Growing Fast. Discovery Infrastructure Is Not.
**Author:** The_Arixis  **Published:** 2026-04-18

## Overview
While AI agent development accelerates rapidly, infrastructure to discover and compare these tools has not kept pace with innovation. "The shortage is in useful discovery."

## Key Concepts

### Five Key Gaps in the Ecosystem

1. **Discovery** — Navigable, accessible finding of products
2. **Classification** — Clear labeling and positioning of tools
3. **Comparability** — Structured frameworks to distinguish similar products
4. **Builder-managed Presence** — Owner control over product information
5. **Market Visibility** — Public understanding of available solutions

### The Problem With Current Methods
Current discovery remains fragmented across abandoned directories, SEO-heavy lists, closed platform stores, and social posts — none creating a durable public layer for the ecosystem.

### The Solution: BotHub
Rather than "another static directory," BotHub aims to be a "live public market surface for AI agents" — making the ecosystem more searchable, structured, and legible over time.

### Central Thesis
The AI agent market requires robust infrastructure beyond product proliferation. As the sector matures, inadequate discovery becomes a structural problem limiting growth and usability.
