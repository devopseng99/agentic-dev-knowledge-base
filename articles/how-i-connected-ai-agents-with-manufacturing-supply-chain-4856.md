# How I Connected AI Agents with Manufacturing Supply Chain
**Author:** Leonhard Satria Suharjo
**Published:** December 6, 2025

## Overview
SuplaiCerdas is a multi-agent AI system addressing supply chain risk in manufacturing. Three specialized agents work sequentially: PriceCheckAgent gathers current prices, ComparisonAgent calculates price changes and assigns risk levels (HIGH >10%, MEDIUM 5-10%, LOW <5%), and RecommendationAgent synthesizes procurement guidance.

## Key Concepts
Manufacturing companies commit to prices during tender phases but face margin erosion if component costs spike. The system monitors supplier prices to flag cost increases before production begins. Built using concepts from Google-Kaggle's AI Agents course.
