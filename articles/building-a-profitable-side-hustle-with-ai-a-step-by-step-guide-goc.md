# Building a Passive Income with AI
**Author:** Peter Mbanugo  **Published:** December 23, 2024

## Overview
Promotes a 90-minute video guide on leveraging AI to create a profitable side business. Outlines strategies for identifying niche markets, using AI ethically for content creation that ranks in search results, establishing authority, and monetizing websites.

## Key Concepts

- Niche identification and market research
- Ethical AI implementation for content generation
- SEO optimization and search ranking strategies
- Building authority in chosen markets
- Monetization frameworks
- Digital entrepreneurship methodology

**Core Principles**
1. Find a hungry niche with search intent
2. Use AI to produce high-quality content at scale
3. Build topical authority through consistent publishing
4. Monetize through ads, affiliates, or products
5. Reinvest in expanding content and niches

**Important Caveat**
This approach "is not for you" if seeking "'get rich quick' hacks" or relying on "lazy use of AI that result in poor outcome for your business in the long term." Genuine effort is required.
