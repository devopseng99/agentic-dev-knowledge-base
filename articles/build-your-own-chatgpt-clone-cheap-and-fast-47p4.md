# Build Your Own ChatGPT Clone - Cheap and Fast
**Author:** Criselda Gomez
**Published:** November 11, 2025

## Overview
Affordable AI chatbot development. GPT-3.5-turbo at ~$0.001/message, or local Ollama. Under $5/month operation.

## Key Concepts
- Tech Stack: Next.js/React frontend, Node.js+Express or Python+FastAPI backend
- AI Options: GPT-3.5-turbo, Claude 3 Haiku, or local Ollama
- Hosting: Render, Railway, or Vercel (free/low-cost)
- Enhancements: conversation memory, persona systems, file uploads, voice, embeddings
