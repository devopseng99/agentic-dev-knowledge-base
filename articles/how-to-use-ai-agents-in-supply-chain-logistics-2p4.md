# How to Use AI Agents in Supply Chain & Logistics
**Author:** Can Mingir
**Published:** July 31, 2025

## Overview
AI Agents are autonomous LLM versions designed for decision-making in enterprise systems. Unlike traditional microservices, they excel at handling complex tasks by decomposing them into manageable steps. Supply chain operations depend heavily on numerous business rules that vary by customer and shift due to seasonal demand, market fluctuations, and regulatory updates.

## Key Concepts
Traditional coding works best for deterministic requirements with few complex rules but demands high maintenance. AI Agents shine when numerous basic rules require frequent updates, providing dynamic adaptability without heavy developer intervention.
