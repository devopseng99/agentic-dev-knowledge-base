# How do AI video generation models work?
**Author:** Nikita Namjoshi (Google AI)  **Published:** April 14, 2026

## Overview
This article explores the mechanics of state-of-the-art video generation models. The piece examines what actually happens when you type a prompt and get back a video clip, breaking down the technical architecture behind these systems.

## Key Concepts

1. The diffusion process
2. Achieving temporal consistency
3. Computational efficiency and autoencoders

Part 2 of a "Release Notes Explained" series. Part 1 covered: "What is an LLM actually doing when it's 'thinking'?"

The article addresses key challenges in video generation:
- Temporal consistency: ensuring objects/characters look the same across frames
- Autoencoder compression for computational efficiency
- Dataset scaling as potential direction for advancement
