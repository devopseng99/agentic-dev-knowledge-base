# How I Use AI to Build Frontend Apps: My Candid, Messy Process
**Author:** Rizel Scarlett
**Published:** November 4, 2025

## Overview
Practical workflow for AI-assisted frontend development: brainstorming with Claude, UI design with Loveable, and rapid iteration with goose AI agent. AI accelerates ideation, not quality -- maintaining editorial control is essential.

## Key Concepts
- Three-phase process: brainstorm (Claude), design (Loveable), iterate (goose agent)
- AI accelerates ideation, not guaranteed quality
- Must maintain editorial control, disagree with AI when necessary
- Completed full project (voice control, gesture recognition) in half a day vs months
