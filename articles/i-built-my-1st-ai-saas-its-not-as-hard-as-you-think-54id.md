# I Built My 1st AI SaaS, It's Not as Hard as You Think
**Author:** Syakir
**Published:** May 27, 2025

## Overview
Kattalog: AI product photography SaaS replacing backgrounds with studio quality. Built in ~3 months with Cursor AI editor.

## Key Concepts
- AI models from Replicate and Fal.ai for image processing
- Frontend: Astro, React, TailwindCSS, Shadcn/Radix, Konva.js
- Backend: PostgreSQL, Drizzle ORM, BetterAuth, Cloudflare R2
- Deployment: VPS with self-hosted Coolify
- Payment: Polar.sh (Stripe alternative)
- Key insight: "The real challenge is product market fit, not development"
