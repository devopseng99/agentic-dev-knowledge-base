# AI in Healthcare: How LLMs are Transforming Medical Documentation and Decision Making
**Author:** Aun Raza  **Published:** September 4, 2025

## Overview
Examines how large language models are revolutionizing healthcare through automation and decision support. Covers transformative applications of LLMs across documentation, clinical guidance, and personalized medicine, while acknowledging persistent challenges around privacy, bias, and regulatory compliance.

## Key Concepts
- Automated Documentation: LLMs generate summaries and clinical notes from dictation, reducing administrative overhead
- Clinical Decision Support: systems analyze patient information and medical literature to suggest evidence-based diagnoses and treatments
- Medical Literature Summarization: models condense complex research papers, enabling clinicians to stay current
- Patient Education: LLMs create personalized materials and answer patient questions
- Drug Discovery: models analyze genomic and clinical trial data to identify potential therapeutic candidates
- Core Technical Features: natural language understanding, knowledge representation, reasoning capabilities, personalization
- Critical Challenges: data protection, algorithmic bias, explainability, system integration, regulatory oversight
