# S1: My Aurora Robotics 2.0 Experience
**Author:** Abdullateef OGUNDIPE  **Published:** April 4, 2026

## Overview
The author documents their first day of Aurora Robotics 2.0 training after realizing that robotics represents the convergence of their interests in control systems, artificial intelligence, and cloud computing. Despite holding a doctoral degree focused on multi-agent coordination, they recognized gaps in foundational knowledge and sought structured learning.

## Key Concepts
- Robotics as intersection of electrical engineering, AI, and cloud technologies
- Model predictive control for multi-agent systems
- Theory-to-implementation gap in robotics education
- ROS2 and programming fundamentals (Python, C++)
- Learning progression: concepts -> mathematics -> code -> simulation -> real-world application
- Mindset priorities: curiosity over perfection, failing fast, consistency, collaboration
