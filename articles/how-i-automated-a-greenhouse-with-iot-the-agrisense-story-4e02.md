# How I Automated a Greenhouse with IoT: The AgriSense Story
**Author:** itsdchanda
**Published:** 2025

## Overview
AgriSense is a smart assistant for greenhouses that monitors temperature, humidity, and other environmental factors while automating controls like fans, heaters, and sprinklers using IoT sensors and AI-driven decision making.

## Key Concepts
Combines IoT sensor networks with intelligent automation to maintain optimal growing conditions, demonstrating practical AI application in controlled environment agriculture.
