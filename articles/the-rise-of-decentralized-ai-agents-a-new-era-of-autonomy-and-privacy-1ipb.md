# The Rise of Decentralized AI Agents: A New Era of Autonomy and Privacy
**Author:** Vishal Singh
**Published:** May 26, 2025

## Overview
Introduces decentralized AI agents as autonomous, self-executing AI models operating on blockchain networks, covering DeFi, healthcare, supply chain, and gaming applications with challenges around scalability and regulation.

## Key Concepts

### Definition
Autonomous, self-executing AI models operating on blockchain networks or decentralized computing frameworks, not controlled by centralized entities.

### Key Features
- Autonomy: Operation without human intervention
- Privacy: Decentralized data processing
- Transparency: Blockchain ledger recording
- Interoperability: Works with DeFi, IoT, smart contracts

### Applications
- DeFi: Trading optimization
- Healthcare: Secure medical record processing
- Supply Chain: Fraud detection and logistics
- Gaming/Metaverse: NPC intelligence and autonomous economies

### Challenges
Scalability, computational efficiency, and regulatory acceptance. Layer 2 solutions, zero-knowledge proofs, and federated learning show promise.
