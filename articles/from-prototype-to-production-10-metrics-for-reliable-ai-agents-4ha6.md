# From Prototype to Production: 10 Metrics for Reliable AI Agents
**Author:** Navya Yadav  **Published:** November 27, 2025

## Overview
Addresses the critical gap between AI agent prototypes and production deployments. "70-85% of AI initiatives fail to meet expected outcomes in production," highlighting the necessity of systematic measurement frameworks for monitoring agent reliability at scale.

## Key Concepts
1. **Task Completion Rate** — Measuring percentage of user requests resolved without human intervention
2. **Accuracy and Error Rate** — Quantifying correctness and severity-weighted failure modes
3. **Latency and Response Time** — Tracking median and 95th percentile performance metrics
4. **Cost Per Transaction** — Monitoring computational expenses across operations
5. **System Uptime and Availability** — Targeting 99.9%+ reliability for business-critical workflows
6. **Regression Detection Rate** — Catching quality degradation before production deployment
7. **Drift and Anomaly Detection** — Identifying behavioral deviations from expected patterns
8. **Security and Compliance Violations** — Tracking vulnerabilities and regulatory adherence
9. **User Satisfaction and Adoption Rate** — Measuring engagement and acceptance levels
10. **Deployment Frequency and Rollback Rate** — Assessing CI/CD maturity and release safety
