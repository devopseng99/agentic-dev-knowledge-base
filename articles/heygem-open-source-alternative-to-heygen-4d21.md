# HeyGem - Open Source Alternative to Heygen
**Author:** HeyGem
**Published:** May 14, 2025

## Overview
Free, open-source AI avatar video synthesis tool by DUIX.com. Creates personalized digital humans entirely offline on Windows.

## Key Concepts
- Voice and appearance cloning from 10-second video
- Text/audio to lip-synced avatar videos with natural facial expressions
- Multi-language: English, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, French, German, Arabic, Spanish
- Offline operation for privacy-conscious environments
- Voice cloning with emotion/intonation replication
- Computer vision for accurate lip-sync and motion tracking
