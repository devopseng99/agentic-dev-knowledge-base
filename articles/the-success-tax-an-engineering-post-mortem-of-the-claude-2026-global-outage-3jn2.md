# The Success Tax: An Engineering Post-Mortem of the Claude 2026 Global Outage
**Author:** Genie Infotech
**Published:** 2026

## Overview
Engineering post-mortem analyzing the Claude 2026 global outage, examining how rapid success and scaling can create infrastructure vulnerabilities. Explores the "success tax" concept where growth outpaces reliability engineering.

## Key Concepts

The article examines cascading failures in AI infrastructure at enterprise scale, lessons about resilience engineering when services grow faster than their reliability layers, and the importance of failover mechanisms and graceful degradation in AI service delivery. Addresses multi-region deployment challenges and the gap between theoretical capacity planning and production reality.
