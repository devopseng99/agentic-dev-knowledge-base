# Launching FocusSync
**Author:** Medea
**Published:** October 21, 2023

## Overview
FocusSync is a community-driven productivity website for setting goals, tracking time, and maintaining accountability through social features. Built as a hackathon submission.

## Key Concepts
- Goal setting with social accountability
- Time tracking for schedule optimization
- Community leaderboards for motivation
- Next.js and React frontend
- Deployed on Vercel
