# Exploring the Benefits of AI in Agriculture
**Author:** Somya Ranjan Acharya
**Published:** February 1, 2024

## Overview
Examines how AI is transforming modern farming through precision farming techniques using data analytics and drones, AI-powered pest detection, automated machinery, yield prediction models, and intelligent water management systems.

## Key Concepts
Addresses implementation challenges including high costs and infrastructure needs while emphasizing AI can help meet growing food demands while reducing environmental impact.
