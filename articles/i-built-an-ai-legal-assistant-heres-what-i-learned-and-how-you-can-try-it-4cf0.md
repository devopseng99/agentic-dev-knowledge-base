# I Built an AI Legal Assistant — Here's What I Learned (and How You Can Try It)
**Author:** Stefan Utu  **Published:** June 22, 2025

## Overview
Stefan identified a gap: "Lawyers are expensive. Legal language is confusing." AI Legal Helper combines artificial intelligence with legal templates to democratize legal assistance. Available at $9.99/month.

## Key Concepts

**Problem Statement**
Legal services are costly and inaccessible to average people. Complex legal documents are incomprehensible to most users, leading to uninformed decisions about contracts that significantly affect rights and obligations.

**What AI Legal Helper Does**
- Contract drafting (NDAs, leases, freelance agreements)
- Plain-English responses to legal questions
- Document review and explanation
- 24/7 availability

**Technical Stack**
- Frontend: React with Tailwind CSS
- Backend: Supabase (authentication, database)
- AI Engine: OpenAI's GPT for processing and generation
- Hosting: Cloudflare Pages

**Key Lessons Learned**
1. "Small pain points matter" in product development — focus on what frustrates real users
2. Legal compliance allows innovation when focused on assistance rather than licensed representation
3. User experience is critical when addressing stressed users needing legal help

**Important Note**
Focused on assistance, not licensed legal representation. Operates in a regulatory space that requires careful framing: "We help you understand, not advise you."
