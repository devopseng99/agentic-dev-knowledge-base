# Which AI Agent Frameworks to Choose for Building Your First AI Agent (2025 Guide)

**Author:** Meenakshi Agarwal
**Published:** June 8, 2025

## Overview
AI agent-based projects grew by 380% over the past year (GitHub Trends 2025). This guide covers six frameworks for building your first AI agent.

## Key Concepts

### Frameworks Reviewed
1. **LangChain** - Most accessible for beginners, Python-based, best for chatbots and Q&A
2. **AutoGen (Microsoft)** - Multi-agent collaboration, ideal for research and report generation
3. **CrewAI** - Lightweight, team-based workflows with different roles
4. **AgentLite** - Minimalistic, designed for rapid prototyping
5. **MetaGPT** - Specialized for software engineering, emulating dev team structures
6. **OpenAgents** - Community-driven, focusing on tool integration

### Selection Criteria
Evaluate based on ease of use, documentation quality, community support, use-case alignment, and extensibility.
