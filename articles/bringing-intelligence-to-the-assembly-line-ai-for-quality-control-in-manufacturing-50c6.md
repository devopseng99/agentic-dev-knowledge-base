# Bringing Intelligence to the Assembly Line: AI for Quality Control in Manufacturing
**Author:** Priyansh Shah
**Published:** May 13, 2025

## Overview
Examines how convolutional neural networks detect defects in manufacturing, integration strategies with factory cameras and edge devices, and how AI outperforms traditional manual inspection for Industry 4.0 operations.

## Key Concepts
Covers predictive analytics for identifying potential faults early in production processes and deployment of CV models on edge devices for real-time inspection.
