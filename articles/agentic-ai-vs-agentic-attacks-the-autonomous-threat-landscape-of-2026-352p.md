# Agentic AI vs. Agentic Attacks: The Autonomous Threat Landscape of 2026
**Author:** CyberPath
**Published:** 2026

## Overview
Unlike traditional malware that follows predictable patterns, AI agents can rapidly modify their behavior to evade detection, rendering static security rules ineffective. Examines both defensive and offensive use of AI agents.

## Key Concepts
Covers the arms race between agentic AI defenses and agentic AI attacks, including adaptive malware, autonomous phishing, and AI-driven social engineering.
