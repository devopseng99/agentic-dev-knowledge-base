# The Rise of Collaborative AI: Exploring Microsoft's AutoGen 0.4, Magnetic-One, and TinyTroupe
**Author:** Naseeb
**Published:** May 15, 2025

## Overview
Examination of three Microsoft multi-agent frameworks: AutoGen 0.4 for conversational AI, Magnetic-One for knowledge sharing via graphs, and TinyTroupe for edge-optimized agents.

## Key Concepts
- AutoGen 0.4: Multi-agent conversational systems with agent abstractions and customizable workflows
- Magnetic-One: Graph-based knowledge sharing and semantic search between agents
- TinyTroupe: Model quantization and edge-optimized communication for resource-constrained devices
