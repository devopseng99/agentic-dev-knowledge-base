# Code Once, Earn Forever: 5 AI-Driven Passive Income Streams for Devs in 2026
**Author:** Khurram Ali Khan  **Published:** December 24, 2025

## Overview
Strategies for developers to generate recurring revenue through AI-powered products and services. It emphasizes that "passive" income requires significant upfront effort but delivers long-term returns with minimal maintenance.

## Key Concepts

1. **Agentic Micro-SaaS** – Building AI agents for niche business problems (e.g., Slack bots converting threads to Jira tickets) leveraging existing app store ecosystems
2. **Digital Exhaust Boilerplates** – Packaging development configurations as starter kits for other developers
3. **API-as-a-Product** – Single-purpose APIs hosted on marketplaces using serverless infrastructure
4. **Fine-Tuning Datasets** – Curating specialized datasets for AI model training and licensing them on platforms like Hugging Face
5. **Faceless Dev Tutorials** – AI-generated video content for technical education with ad revenue and affiliate monetization

**Key Insight**
"Passive is a bit of a lie at the start. It's a front-loaded effort. You spend 40 hours building the asset so you can spend 1 hour a month maintaining it."
