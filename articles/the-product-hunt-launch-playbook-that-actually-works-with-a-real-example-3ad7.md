# The Product Hunt Launch Playbook That Actually Works (With a Real Example)
**Author:** Atlas Whoff  **Published:** 2026-04-07

## Overview
Proven tactics for a successful Product Hunt launch, with a real example from whoffagents.com.

## Key Concepts

### Core Timing Strategy
Launch on Tuesday-Thursday at 12:01 AM PST to maximize the 24-hour ranking window. "Weekend launches die because the PH community is smaller."

### Pre-Launch Phase (2 weeks)

**Week 1 - Audience Building:**
- Post on Twitter every 2-3 days about your product
- Share progress publicly
- Collect interested emails
- Recruit a "hunter" if needed for amplification

**Week 2 - Asset Preparation:**
- Create clean hero image, 3-4 product screenshots, optional demo GIF
- Write specific taglines (avoid "revolutionary" or "AI-powered")
- Craft authentic origin story in full description

### Launch Day Timeline

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| 12:01 AM | Product goes live with auto-posted first comment |
| 12:01-2:00 AM | Email and text outreach to network |
| 6:00-9:00 AM | Twitter announcement push (3 coordinated tweets) |
| All day | Respond to comments within 30 minutes |
| 6:00 PM-Midnight | Final momentum update post |

### Critical First Comment Formula
Who you are → why you built it → 2-3 proud features → invitation for questions. Personal narrative over marketing copy.

### Launch Killers
- Directly asking for upvotes
- Launching with zero audience
- Ignoring comments
- Vague positioning statements
- Shipping incomplete products

### Post-Launch Value
Successful launches create SEO benefits that compound for months through keyword-rich PH listing rankings and backlinks.
