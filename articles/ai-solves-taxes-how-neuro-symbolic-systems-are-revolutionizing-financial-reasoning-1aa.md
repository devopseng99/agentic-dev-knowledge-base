# AI Solves Taxes? How Neuro-Symbolic Systems Are Revolutionizing Financial Reasoning
**Author:** Arvind Sundara Rajan
**Published:** September 1, 2025

## Overview
Explores how neuro-symbolic systems combining neural networks with symbolic logic address limitations of traditional AI in financial reasoning. Encodes tax rules as formal logic while leveraging LLMs for natural language translation and case retrieval.

## Key Concepts
The hybrid approach delivers improved accuracy, enhanced auditability, and increased accessibility to financial services, potentially democratizing personalized financial advice across income levels.
