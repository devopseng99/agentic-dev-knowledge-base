# I built a self-hosted AI agent that markets itself. Here's how.
**Author:** Fahad R
**Published:** May 1, 2026

## Overview
Daemora: self-hosted AI agent with 52 built-in tools, 60 skills, 20 messaging channels, 7 AI providers, and autonomous marketing capabilities.

## Key Concepts
- 52 tools and 60 skills out of the box
- Three-layer memory: semantic, episodic, procedural
- 14-layer security with AES-256-GCM encryption
- 20 channels: Telegram, Discord, Slack, WhatsApp, etc.
- 7 AI providers including local Ollama
- MCP integration: GitHub, Notion, Linear, Postgres
- Autonomous growth campaign: competitor monitoring, social posts, video generation
- Tech: Node.js 20+, Vercel AI SDK, SQLite, Remotion, Twilio, ElevenLabs
