# Agentic AI: How LLMs Really Work Behind the Scenes

**Author:** Gayathri Mangalarapu
**Published:** 2026

## Overview
Explores how LLMs work behind the scenes in agentic AI systems, covering the architecture of autonomous agents that can reason, plan, and act independently.

## Key Concepts

Agentic AI represents the evolution from passive LLM responses to active, autonomous systems that:
- Reason about problems and decompose them into steps
- Plan sequences of actions to achieve goals
- Use tools and APIs to interact with external systems
- Learn from observations and adjust their approach
- Operate within defined boundaries and safety constraints

The key architectural insight is that the LLM serves as the reasoning engine within a larger system that provides memory, tools, planning capabilities, and feedback loops.
