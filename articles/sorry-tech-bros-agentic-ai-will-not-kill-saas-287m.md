# Sorry tech bros, Agentic AI will not kill SaaS
**Author:** DJ Leamen  **Published:** June 14, 2025

## Overview
Examines whether autonomous AI agents will disrupt or enhance traditional SaaS business models. Concludes that agentic AI will augment rather than replace SaaS, creating hybrid models where AI handles repetitive work while humans manage strategy and relationships.

## Key Concepts

- Agentic AI systems perform multi-step workflows autonomously with minimal human input
- Strengths include 24/7 automation, cross-system orchestration, and adaptability
- Critical weaknesses: lack of transparency, brittleness in edge cases, and need for human oversight
- "Headless SaaS" concept where AI becomes the interface layer while applications remain in background
- Industry applications in marketing, customer service, finance, software development, and healthcare
- Human-in-the-loop remains essential for ethical judgment and creative work

**The Nuanced View**
SaaS will evolve, not die. The smart play is "Headless SaaS" — AI agents become the interface, existing applications remain the reliable execution backbone.
