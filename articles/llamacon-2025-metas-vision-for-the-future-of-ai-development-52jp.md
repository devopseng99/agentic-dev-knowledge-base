# LlamaCon 2025: Meta's Vision for the Future of AI Development
**Author:** Naim Ai  **Published:** May 1, 2025

## Overview
Meta held its inaugural LlamaCon on April 29, 2025, unveiling advancements in large language models and developer tools. The event showcased Llama 3 and Llama 4 models, a new Llama API, and multimodal capabilities—positioning Meta as a competitor to OpenAI and Google through open-weight model distribution.

## Key Concepts

- **Llama 3 & 4 Models:** Variants with 8B and 70B parameters; Llama 4 in training with multimodal and multilingual support
- **Mixture of Experts (MoE) Architecture:** Dynamically activates neural network subsets per query to reduce compute costs
- **Extended Context Windows:** Llama 3 supports up to 8K tokens; Llama 4 targets longer sequences
- **Llama API:** Hosted infrastructure for low-latency inference, streaming output, and fine-tuning capabilities
- **Meta AI Assistant:** Embedded across Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp; uses RAG for current information retrieval
- **Code Llama:** Multi-language code generation with IDE integration
- **LlamaIndex:** Framework for document ingestion and RAG pipelines
- **Open-Weight Strategy:** Prioritizes transparency, custom fine-tuning, and on-premises deployment
