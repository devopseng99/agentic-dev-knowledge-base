# Keeping Your Services Up and Running: A Friendly Guide to High Availability
**Author:** lovestaco
**Published:** 2026

## Overview
Practical guide to high availability patterns for keeping services operational during failures. Covers redundancy strategies, load balancing, health checks, failover mechanisms, and the engineering trade-offs between availability targets (99.9% vs 99.99% uptime).

## Key Concepts

High availability fundamentals: redundancy at every layer (compute, storage, network), automated health checks with configurable thresholds, load balancing for traffic distribution, circuit breakers to prevent cascading failures, and graceful degradation when components fail. The guide emphasizes that availability is an architectural decision made upfront, not a feature added later.
