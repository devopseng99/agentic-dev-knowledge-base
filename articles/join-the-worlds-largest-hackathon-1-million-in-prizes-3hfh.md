# Join the World's Largest Hackathon: $1 Million in Prizes
**Author:** Peter Kim Frank
**Published:** May 30, 2025

## Overview
Bolt's World's Largest Hackathon with over $1,000,000 in prizes. Welcomes both experienced developers and first-time entrepreneurs to build production-ready applications using AI.

## Key Concepts
- Registration through DevPost
- Builder pack with thousands of credits to different services (AI voice, UI gen, infra)
- Livestream events: Domain Expertise with Entri, API fundamentals with Pica, Conversational Video Agents workshop
- Writing contest with exclusive DEV badges
- Bolt as AI platform enabling no-code/low-code production apps
