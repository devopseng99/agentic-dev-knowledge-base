# I Built an AI Agent That Cares for My Mom's 15-Medication Health Routine — No Code, Just Love
**Author:** Dr. Afnan  **Published:** July 7, 2025

## Overview
A no-code automation solution for the Runner H "AI Agent Prompting" Challenge addressing real-world healthcare management. The creator developed an intelligent system to coordinate their mother's complex medical routine involving 15+ medications, chronic condition management (diabetes, hypertension, kidney issues), and multi-caregiver coordination using the Runner H platform.

## Key Concepts
- Automated health coordination via daily email notifications
- Medication scheduling across three daily intervals (7 AM, 2 PM, 4 PM, 9 PM)
- Multi-recipient delivery to author and sister (co-caregivers)
- Integrated health monitoring including blood sugar, blood pressure, and lab test tracking
- No-code implementation using Runner H platform
- Emotional support integration alongside clinical reminders
- Weekly laboratory test alerts
- Blood sugar and BP tracking notifications
- Exercise and nutrition guidance
