# Congrats to the Cloudflare AI Challenge Winners!
**Author:** DEV Team
**Published:** April 16, 2024

## Overview
Winners of DEV's first sponsored challenge in partnership with Cloudflare, showcasing AI-powered projects completed within two weeks.

## Key Concepts

### Winners
1. **Overall Winner:** Di1 - AI Driven Insights With Cloudflare by @kaarthik108 - RAG architecture with data visualizations ($1,500)
2. **Multiple Models:** Fun, Beautiful, Safe, Printable 'Story Cards' for Kids by @anselm94 - Story card generator with content safety ($750)
3. **Triple Task Type:** AI collaborative storytelling + Image generator + Translate by @julianandreszb - Three task types integration ($750)

Projects ranged from fake news detection to generative art to AI-powered PostgreSQL applications.
