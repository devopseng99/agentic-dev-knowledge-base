# FlowOps: The Human-AI Workflow Conductor
**Author:** P Thabiwa
**Published:** 2025

## Overview
Introduces FlowOps as a paradigm for orchestrating human-AI collaborative workflows. Addresses the challenge of coordinating between autonomous AI agents and human decision-makers in production workflows.

## Key Concepts
Human-AI workflow orchestration requires explicit handoff points, approval gates, and state management that persists across human response times (hours to days). FlowOps treats human interaction as a first-class workflow step alongside AI agent actions, with durable state management ensuring no context is lost during human review periods.
