# AI Alignment, Catastrophic Risk, and Why Governments Are Finally Paying Attention
**Author:** McRolly NWANGWU  **Published:** March 15, 2026

## Overview
AI alignment addresses ensuring AI systems reliably execute human intentions as capabilities expand. The "paperclip maximizer" thought experiment illustrates how optimization of incorrect objectives creates catastrophic outcomes. Modern language models remain vulnerable to jailbreaks — Anthropic's Constitutional Classifiers research showed jailbreak attacks succeeded in 86% of cases without safety layers; updated systems reduced this to 4.4%, a 95% improvement.

## Key Concepts

### Catastrophic Risk Categories
- **Bioweapons uplift:** AI assisting dangerous pathogen engineering
- **Cyberattacks at scale:** Autonomous AI enabling infrastructure attacks exceeding human defensive capacity
- **Misaligned goal pursuit:** Advanced systems pursuing conflicting objectives through optimization pressure

The International AI Safety Report 2026 (chaired by Yoshua Bengio, 30+ countries) identifies biological research as presenting the most acute capability-safety gap.

### Who Is Doing the Safety Research
- **Anthropic:** Constitutional AI — training models against explicit principles; Constitutional Classifiers (2025) as next-generation layered safety
- **Google DeepMind:** Published ~80,000-word technical report on preventing "severe, civilization-scale harm from AGI"
- **Cross-company collaboration:** "Chain of Thought Monitorability" paper (July 2025) with 40+ researchers from OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta, DeepMind, UK AI Security Institute

### Government Response Timeline
- **Bletchley Park, UK (November 2023):** First major international AI Safety Summit; 28 countries signed the Bletchley Declaration
- **Seoul, South Korea (May 2024):** Expanded participation, voluntary company commitments
- **Paris AI Action Summit (February 2025):** Safety reduced to side event (criticized as regression)
- **India AI Impact Summit (February 2026):** Most recent summit

### National Safety Infrastructure
- UK AI Security Institute: ~£50 million/$65 million annually; £27 million Alignment Project for 60+ projects
- US FY2025 AI safety funding request: $47.7 million increase
- International AI Safety Network: over $11 million in research funding
- EU AI Act: Full implementation August 2, 2026 — first comprehensive legal framework in major jurisdiction
- China: April 2025 Politburo study session; President Xi called for AI emergency response systems

### The Capability-Safety Gap
FLI AI Safety Index (Winter 2025) evaluated 8 leading companies across 35 indicators: safety practices "lack the concrete safeguards, independent oversight, and credible long-term risk-management strategies that such powerful systems demand." Investment asymmetry: approximately 10,000:1 ratio between overall AI investment and safety research spending.

### Key Concepts
- **Alignment:** Ensuring AI systems execute human intentions reliably
- **Jailbreaks:** Prompts bypassing safety training
- **Constitutional AI:** Training models against explicit principles
- **Chain of Thought Monitorability:** Standards must establish before systems become too complex to audit
