# Generative AI in Drug Discovery: Designing Molecules with Precision
**Author:** Siddharth Bhalsod  **Published:** April 30, 2025

## Overview
Examines how generative AI is revolutionizing pharmaceutical research by accelerating drug discovery and enabling precise molecular design. Covers the shift from traditional trial-and-error approaches to AI-guided computational design.

## Key Concepts
- Generative AI Types: GANs, VAEs, RNNs, diffusion models, flow-based models, autoregressive models, transformer-based models
- Molecular Representation: encoding molecular structures into machine-readable formats preserving atomic relationships and bond information
- Multi-parameter Optimization: simultaneous balancing of potency, selectivity, bioavailability, metabolic stability, and safety
- Molecular Fragmentation: breaking complex molecules into meaningful substructures to teach AI chemical "grammar"
- Economic Impact: estimated $110 billion annual value for life sciences sector
- Timeline Acceleration: DSP-1181 development completed in 12 months versus typical 4.5 years
- Multi-agent systems combining different AI technologies for target identification, molecule design, and candidate evaluation
