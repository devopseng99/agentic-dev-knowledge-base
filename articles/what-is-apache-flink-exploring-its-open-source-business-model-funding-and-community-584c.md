# What is Apache Flink? Open Source Business Model and Community
**Author:** kallileiser
**Published:** May 11, 2025

## Overview
Examines Apache Flink as a distributed stream processing framework under Apache 2.0 License. Covers technical capabilities, funding models, and community-driven development.

## Key Concepts
- High throughput, low latency architecture with fault tolerance
- Applications across finance, IoT, and telecommunications
- Funding: volunteer contributions, corporate sponsorships, institutional grants
- Future: integration with AI/ML systems and enhanced ecosystem collaboration
- Challenges: technical complexity and resource intensity for adoption
