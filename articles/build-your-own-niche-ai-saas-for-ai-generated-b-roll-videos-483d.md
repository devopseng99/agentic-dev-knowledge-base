# Build Your Own Niche AI SaaS for AI-Generated B-Roll Videos
**Author:** SevimsOffice
**Published:** November 6, 2025

## Overview
Specialized SaaS for generating B-roll videos from user images. Layered architecture with multiple AI tools.

## Key Concepts
- 6 layers: Foundation -> Data -> AI -> Automation -> Review -> Deployment
- Lovable (frontend), Supabase (backend), Replicate (AI models)
- fastflux trainer for model fine-tuning
- Kling v2.1 for video generation
- Niche targeting over broad market approach
