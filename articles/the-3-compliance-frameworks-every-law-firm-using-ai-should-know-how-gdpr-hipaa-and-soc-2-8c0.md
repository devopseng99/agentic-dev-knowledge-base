# The 3 Compliance Frameworks Every Law Firm Using AI Should Know: GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC 2
**Author:** Josh Lee  **Published:** October 22, 2025

## Overview
For law firms adopting AI, understanding GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC 2 is essential. Compliance also serves as a competitive advantage by demonstrating trustworthiness to privacy-conscious clients.

## Key Concepts

**GDPR (Data Privacy & Rights)**
- Identify what personal data AI tools process
- Obtain clear consent before data collection
- Retain only necessary information
- Enable client access to view, correct, or delete their data
- Implement encryption and access controls
- Maintain documentation of data processing

**HIPAA (Health Information Security)**
For firms handling health-related legal matters:
- Role-based access controls with strong authentication
- Written vendor agreements specifying privacy/security obligations
- Regular staff training on health data protection
- Ongoing monitoring of AI systems for irregularities

**SOC 2 (Cloud Service Controls)**
Focus on five Trust Service Criteria: security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy:
- Request SOC 2 audit reports from vendors
- Establish clear data access policies
- Conduct regular compliance reviews

**Implementation Strategy**
- Create data flow maps showing sensitive information movement
- Train staff on privacy regulations
- Vet vendors for SOC 2 compliance standards

**Key Message:** Compliance is not just a legal obligation — it is a business differentiator.
