# What is Decentralized AI? Understanding the Future of Artificial Intelligence
**Author:** SwarmZero
**Published:** September 11, 2024

## Overview
Explores decentralized AI using blockchain to distribute data across nodes, addressing data security and privacy concerns while enabling users to create, monetize, and combine AI agents.

## Key Concepts

### Advantages Over Centralized AI
- Enhanced security through distributed architecture
- User control over personal data
- Improved scalability
- Transparent decision-making via consensus mechanisms
- Elimination of single points of failure

### SwarmZero Platform
Implements decentralized AI principles, enabling users to create, monetize, and combine AI agents while maintaining transparency and security through blockchain integration.
