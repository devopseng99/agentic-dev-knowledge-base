# Global Shutter Camera USB: MerlinPlus for Low-Bandwidth Vision
**Author:** S M Tahosin  **Published:** April 28, 2026

## Overview
Vadzo Imaging's MerlinPlus series introduces an affordable global shutter camera solution for embedded systems with limited bandwidth. The Merlin-234CGS model combines a 2-megapixel onsemi AR0234 sensor with onboard dewarping and USB 2.0 connectivity, eliminating motion artifacts common in rolling shutter designs while reducing computational demands on host systems.

## Key Concepts
- Global shutter vs. rolling shutter capture methodology
- Onboard image dewarping and processing offloading
- UVC (USB Video Class) compliance for plug-and-play integration
- 1/2.7-inch optical format, 2.3-megapixel sensor
- S-Mount lens compatibility and GPIO synchronization
- Target applications: robotics, AGVs, drones, medical devices
