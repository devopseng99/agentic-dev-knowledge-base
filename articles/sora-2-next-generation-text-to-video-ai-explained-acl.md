# Sora 2: Next Generation Text-to-Video AI Explained
**Author:** Ali Farhat  **Published:** September 30, 2025

## Overview
OpenAI's Sora 2 represents a significant advancement in AI-driven video generation, extending clip duration to 30–60 seconds, improving visual fidelity, and enhancing temporal consistency. While designed for content creators, it has practical applications across marketing, education, product demos, and enterprise automation workflows when integrated thoughtfully.

## Key Concepts

- **Diffusion-based generative modeling** – noise gradually denoised through transformer-based attention
- **Latent space compression** – frames encoded into smaller representations for efficiency
- **Temporal consistency** – reduced flickering and improved object permanence
- **Physics realism** – more convincing motion and collision handling
- **Governance frameworks** – necessary safeguards including watermarking, bias audits, and human review

Notable Risks: Artifacts, physics errors, documented bias issues, IP concerns, deepfake misuse potential, and regulatory uncertainty.
