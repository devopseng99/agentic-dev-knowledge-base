# How I Build ERP Software With Django
**Author:** Hossain Chisty  **Published:** September 7, 2022

## Overview
A Django-based ERP platform called Freshdesk designed to help businesses manage operations comprehensively, covering accounts, customers, products, orders, invoices, expense, purchase, report, return, damage, sales, service, stock, suppliers and more.

## Key Concepts

**Core Management Modules**
- Activity Dashboard
- Account Management
- Product Management
- Barcode & QR-Code Sales Integration
- Purchase Management
- Stock Management
- Sales Management
- Service Management
- Expense Management with real-time pie charts
- Damage Management
- Customer Management
- Supplier Management

**Advanced Capabilities**
- Engagement Tracking
- Automated Excel and CSV export generation
- Connected Device Viewing
- Device Activity Tracking
- Automated PDF Report Generation
- Data Download/Export functionality (48-hour processing)
- Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for admin access

The project is open-source and available on GitHub.
