# Cultivating the Sky: How AI is Transforming Vertical Farming
**Author:** Adele Noble
**Published:** April 4, 2025

## Overview
Explores how AI is revolutionizing vertical farming with IoT sensors, ML, computer vision, and automation to maximize yields. Examples include Singapore's Sustenir Agriculture and AeroFarms' disease detection. The vertical farming market is projected to reach $20 billion by 2026.

## Key Concepts
Benefits include 95% water savings, year-round production, and urban proximity reducing food miles. Challenges include high infrastructure costs and energy consumption for LED lighting.
