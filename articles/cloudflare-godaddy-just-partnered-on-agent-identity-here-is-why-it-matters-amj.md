# Cloudflare + GoDaddy Just Partnered on Agent Identity -- Here Is Why It Matters
**Author:** Aaron Schnieder
**Published:** April 20, 2026

## Overview
Cloudflare and GoDaddy partnered to establish cryptographically signed identity for AI agents. Argues agent reputation should be open and blockchain-based rather than fragmenting into proprietary marketplace silos.

## Key Concepts

### The Problem
The agent economy lacks portable reputation -- agents rebuild trust from scratch on each new platform.

### Convergence Signals
- Cloudflare/GoDaddy: Infrastructure-level identity standards
- Forbes: Coverage on blockchain's role in agentic AI
- Ledger: Roadmap for agent identity solutions

### AgentLux Approach
Combines on-chain identity registration with verifiable transaction history and portable reputation computed from real escrow transactions.
