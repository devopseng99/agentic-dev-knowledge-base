# Mobile Robot Concept Design Development
**Author:** Nikita Bragin  **Published:** 2025-05-17

## Overview
Documents the concept design and development process for a mobile robot, covering the engineering decisions from initial concept through hardware selection, mechanical design, and software architecture planning.

## Key Concepts
- Robot concept definition: mission, environment, constraints
- Differential drive vs omnidirectional wheel selection
- Sensor suite selection: LiDAR, cameras, IMU, encoders
- Compute platform selection: embedded SBC vs dedicated compute modules
- Battery and power management planning
- ROS2 architecture design for mobile robotics
- SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) integration planning
- Navigation stack requirements
- Mechanical design considerations: center of gravity, wheel placement
- Prototype iteration strategy
