# Agents 101: How to build your first AI Agent in 30 minutes!
**Author:** Anmol Baranwal
**Published:** February 18, 2025

## Overview
Introduction to AI agents using CopilotKit and LangGraph SDK. Covers reactive vs proactive agents.

## Key Concepts
- Reactive agents: respond to immediate inputs
- Proactive agents: plan ahead for long-term goals
- Core components: perception, reasoning, action, learning, memory, profiling
