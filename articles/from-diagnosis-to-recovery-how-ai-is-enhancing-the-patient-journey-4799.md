# From Diagnosis to Recovery: How AI is Enhancing the Patient Journey
**Author:** sanjay khambhala  **Published:** July 30, 2025

## Overview
Explores how artificial intelligence is transforming healthcare by creating seamless, personalized patient experiences across the entire care continuum—from initial symptom assessment through recovery and rehabilitation.

## Key Concepts
- Smart Symptom Assessment: AI-powered virtual triage systems help patients evaluate symptoms and determine appropriate care levels
- Accelerated Diagnosis: machine learning algorithms support radiologists and clinicians by analyzing medical imaging and lab results
- Personalized Treatment Planning: AI extracts EHR data to predict treatment outcomes and optimize drug dosages
- Continuous Monitoring: remote patient monitoring devices track vital signs and alert providers to concerning trends
- Enhanced Recovery: technologies like MotionAI use sensors and algorithms to track rehabilitation progress and adapt physical therapy protocols in real-time
- Connected Care Experience: seamless information flow across all touchpoints creates cohesive narratives of care
