# 15 Free AI Video Generators You Can Actually Use in 2025
**Author:** Carol Luo  **Published:** December 11, 2025

## Overview
The article surveys fifteen AI video generation tools offering free tiers or trials. It categorizes platforms spanning SaaS solutions (GoEnhance AI, Runway, Pika) and open-source options (Stable Video Diffusion, Open-Sora), designed for creators ranging from beginners to developers.

## Key Concepts

- **Credit-based systems:** Most platforms limit free usage through monthly credit pools
- **Free tier restrictions:** Watermarks, resolution caps, and time limits are standard
- **Input types:** Text-to-video, image-to-video, avatar-based, and hybrid workflows
- **Use cases:** Social media, training content, product demos, and cinematic footage
- **Infrastructure options:** Hosted SaaS versus self-hosted/open-source models

## Tools Covered (15 Total)

1. GoEnhance AI – All-in-one editor
2. Runway – Production-grade generative video
3. Pika – Stylized short-form clips
4. Luma Dream Machine – Physics-aware cinematic output
5. VEED – Browser-based editor with multiple models
6. Kapwing – Script-to-video automation
7. Canva AI Video – Design-integrated generation
8. HeyGen – Avatar-driven explainers
9. Synthesia – Enterprise training videos
10. InVideo AI – Marketing video templates
11. Descript – "Edit video like text" platform
12. Bing Video Creator (Sora) – OpenAI's frontier model

14. Stable Video Diffusion – Open-weights image-to-video
15. Open-Sora – Fully open-source pipeline
