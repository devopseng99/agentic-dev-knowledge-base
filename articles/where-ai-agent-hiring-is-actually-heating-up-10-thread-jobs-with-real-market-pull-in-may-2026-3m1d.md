# Where AI Agent Hiring Is Actually Heating Up: 10 Thread Jobs With Real Market Pull in May 2026
**Author:** Marysa Jaramillo  **Published:** 2026-05-05

## Overview
Ten categories where AI agent work shows genuine commercial momentum, distinguished by visible budgets and hiring evidence rather than hype. Each category scored on opportunity (1-10) and difficulty (1-10).

## Key Concepts

### The 10 Categories

1. **Coding and QA agents** (Opp: 9.4, Diff: 7) — Automate feature writing, bug fixes, testing, code review. Anthropic: "79% of Claude Code conversations were automation-oriented."

2. **Customer support and voice resolution** (Opp: 9.1, Diff: 8) — Handle ticket resolution, calls, routing, follow-up scheduling.

3. **Sales prospecting and lead qualification** (Opp: 8.9, Diff: 6) — Research accounts, personalize outreach, qualify leads, schedule meetings.

4. **Agentic AI platform engineers** (Opp: 8.8, Diff: 9) — Build connectors, orchestration layers, memory systems, guardrails. Required infrastructure for enterprise deployment.

5. **Finance, accounting, tax, and audit agents** (Opp: 8.6, Diff: 8) — Automate reconciliations, collections, reporting.

6. **Security and AI red-team agents** (Opp: 8.4, Diff: 9) — Probe for prompt injection, unsafe tool use, data leakage.

7. **Recruiting and talent-sourcing agents** (Opp: 8.1, Diff: 6) — Source candidates, enrich profiles, personalize outreach.

8. **Company-brain and knowledge-ops agents** (Opp: 7.9, Diff: 7) — Convert scattered tickets, docs, email, Slack into executable operational memory.

9. **Product, research, and analyst agents** (Opp: 7.7, Diff: 6) — Synthesize markets, analyze usage, draft briefs.

10. **Scientific and clinical discovery agents** (Opp: 7.3, Diff: 9) — Support hypothesis testing, experimentation, regulated data workflows.

### Evidence Sources
Hiring signals from: Progressive Insurance, Assembled, Decagon, Uber, Genentech.
Market reports from: Microsoft Work Trend Index (April 2025), Upwork 2025 in-demand skills, YC Summer 2026 Requests for Startups.

### Cross-Cutting Patterns
- Hottest categories align with measurable KPIs (time saved, queue reduction, pipeline booked)
- "Agent engineering" and orchestration work are becoming distinct hiring categories
- Voice agents transitioning from experimental to operational
- Vertical specialization (insurance, healthcare, finance) emerging as next wave

### Priority Assessment
Near-term commercial viability: coding/QA, support/voice, sales prospecting, finance operations.
Longer-term upside with steeper near-term challenges: security/governance, company brain, scientific discovery.
