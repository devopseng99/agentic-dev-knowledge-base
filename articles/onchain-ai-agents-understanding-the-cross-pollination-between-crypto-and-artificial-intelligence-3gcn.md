# Onchain AI Agents: Understanding the Cross-Pollination between Crypto and Artificial Intelligence
**Author:** Aguchukwu Emmanuel
**Published:** December 3, 2024

## Overview
Explores the frontier of onchain AI agents, from Truth Terminal becoming the first AI agent millionaire to projects like ARMA, Virtuals Protocol, and AI16z Eliza building the onchain AI revolution.

## Key Concepts

### How AI Agents Work
Four-stage operational cycle: Perception (gathering data), Input Processing (ML analysis), Decision Making (forming conclusions), Action (executing decisions).

### Truth Terminal
Created by Andy Ayrey. Released Infinite Backrooms recording conversations between two Claude-3 Opus instances. Received $50,000 bitcoin grant from Marc Andreessen. Goatseus Maximus ($GOAT) meme coin launched on Solana reaching $950 million market cap in two weeks.

### Projects Building the Revolution
- **ARMA by Giza**: Autonomous yield optimization agent for Mode Network using account abstraction
- **Virtuals Protocol**: Co-ownership layer for AI agents on Base Blockchain with multimodal autonomous agents
- **AI16z Eliza**: TypeScript-based multi-agent simulation framework with Discord, Twitter/X, Telegram integration

### Concerns
- Scalability: Layer 1 blockchains not designed for millions of multi-step AI agent transactions
- Fragmentation: Limited cross-chain compatibility
- Infrastructure: Most blockchain infrastructure tailored for human users
