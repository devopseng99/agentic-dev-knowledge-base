# FarmSense AI - An intelligent farming companion
**Author:** Prath
**Published:** April 19, 2026

## Overview
FarmSense AI is a full-stack precision agriculture platform providing small and mid-size farmers with expert-level agronomic guidance through three AI modules: Crop Doctor (disease diagnosis via image upload), Irrigation Advisor (weather-integrated scheduling), and Soil Health Advisor (crop rotation and composting guidance). Uses Google Gemini 2.5 Flash and Open-Meteo weather data.

## Key Concepts
Built with Next.js 15, React 19, Express, TypeScript, Prisma, PostgreSQL, and LangChain. Features automatic GPS detection, streaming responses, expense extraction with finance tracking, and proactive weather alerts. Deployed on Netlify (frontend) and Railway (backend).
