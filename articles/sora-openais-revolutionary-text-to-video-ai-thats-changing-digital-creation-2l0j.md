# Sora: OpenAI's Revolutionary Text-to-Video AI That's Changing Digital Creation
**Author:** ANIRUDDHA ADAK  **Published:** December 14, 2024

## Overview
The article introduces Sora, OpenAI's text-to-video generation model, explaining its capabilities as a tool for creating realistic videos from text descriptions. It functions similarly to DALL-E but for video content, utilizing diffusion model technology to transform noise into coherent visual scenes.

## Key Concepts

1. **Core Technology:** Uses diffusion models that "start with static noise and gradually transform it by removing the noise over many steps"
2. **Technical Capabilities:**
   - Video generation up to 1080p resolution
   - Content creation up to 20 seconds in duration
   - Still image-to-video transformation
   - Multi-shot video generation within single projects
3. **Unique Strengths:** Deep understanding of physical world interactions and character consistency across scenes
4. **Current Limitations:** Struggles with complex physics simulations and extended narrative coherence
5. **Safety Measures:** Content moderation, deepfake prevention, and controlled access through ChatGPT Plus ($20/month) and Pro ($200/month) tiers
