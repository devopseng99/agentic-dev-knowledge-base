# 10 Powerful Open Source AI Tools You Won't Believe Are Still Free in 2026
**Author:** Firethering
**Published:** November 3, 2025

## Overview
Ten free open-source AI tools covering browser automation, local LLMs, image/video processing, and more.

## Key Concepts

### Tools
1. **BrowserOS** - AI browser automation
2. **Everywhere AI** - Context-aware desktop assistant
3. **Jan AI** - Local LLM desktop app
4. **Upscayl** - Image upscaling
5. **Stable Diffusion WebUI** - Generative art
6. **Video2X** - Video upscaling
7. **Kdenlive** - Video editor with AI motion tracking
8. **Meshroom** - 3D photogrammetry
9. **AnythingLLM** - Document-based LLM
10. **WinSTT** - Offline speech-to-text
