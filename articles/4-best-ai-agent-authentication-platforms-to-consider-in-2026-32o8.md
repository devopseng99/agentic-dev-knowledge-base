# 4 Best AI Agent Authentication platforms to consider in 2026

**Author:** Aakash R (Composio)
**Published:** February 3, 2026

## Overview
Comparison of four authentication platforms for AI agents: Composio, Merge, Arcade, and Nango.

## Key Concepts

### Platform Comparison

**Composio** - 500+ integrations, managed AgentAuth, structured actions. Best for agent-first products.

**Merge (Agent Handler)** - Enterprise governance focus, scoped permissions, audit trails. Best for regulated environments.

**Arcade** - Action-level authorization before execution. Best for high-risk/irreversible actions.

**Nango** - OAuth/credential management specialist. User-delegated access with secure token storage. Auth only, no execution.

### Decision Framework
Platforms handling authentication + execution + reliability together reduce long-term maintenance vs assembled solutions.
