# Steering the Future: An Introduction to Artificial Intelligence Alignment
**Author:** Brylie Christopher Oxley  **Published:** May 31, 2023

## Overview
AI alignment is research focused on ensuring AI system behavior is in line with human values. Two critical components: designing systems that pursue human goals (value alignment) and building systems that comprehend human intentions (interpretation). As AI integrates into healthcare, finance, transportation, and entertainment, ensuring these systems operate according to human values becomes paramount.

## Key Concepts

### Why Alignment Matters
- **Safety:** Misaligned systems risk unintentional harm despite benign intentions
- **Ethics:** AI making decisions affecting human lives must reflect ethical standards
- **Long-term Future:** Advanced general AI could create severe consequences from misalignment

### Challenges
1. **Complexity of Human Values:** Values remain complex, context-dependent, and often implicit — resisting straightforward encoding
2. **Value Extrapolation:** Systems must adapt as societal values evolve over time
3. **Diverse Perspectives:** Humanity lacks universal value agreement, complicating universally acceptable system design

### Current Research Strategies

**Inverse Reinforcement Learning (IRL):** Technique inferring goals from observed behavior, applicable to extracting human values for implementation.

**Cooperative Inverse Reinforcement Learning (CIRL):** Extension treating humans and AI as part of the same team, collaborating to achieve desired goals.

**Interpretability Research:** Increasing AI decision transparency enables human correction of misalignment.

**Debate and Amplification:** Multiple systems debate questions or iteratively improve responses under human oversight.

### Conclusion
Alignment serves as humanity's "guide star" for navigating artificial intelligence advancement. Defining our values, taking measured actions, and doing careful research enables building beneficial AI. References: Russell, Hadfield-Menell, Amodei, Anthropic, OpenAI, Irving.
