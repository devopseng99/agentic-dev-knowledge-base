# AI Agents in Manufacturing: A New Era of Excellence
**Author:** Bruce Wayne
**Published:** January 30, 2025

## Overview
Explores how AI agents transform manufacturing through IoT sensors, machine learning, NLP, robotics, and computer vision. Applications include predictive maintenance, quality control, supply chain optimization, autonomous robotics, energy management, and demand forecasting.

## Key Concepts
AI agents optimize production processes, reduce downtime, and enhance speed. Manufacturers adopting these technologies gain competitive advantages through enhanced productivity and operational resilience.
