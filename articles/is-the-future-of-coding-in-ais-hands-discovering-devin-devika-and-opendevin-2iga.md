# Meet the New AI Engineers: Devin, Devika, and OpenDevin
**Author:** BekahHW (OpenSauced)
**Published:** April 4, 2024

## Overview
Examines three AI software engineering systems: Devin (by Cognition Labs), Devika (open-source alternative by Mufeed VH), and OpenDevin (community replication).

## Key Concepts

### Devin (Cognition Labs)
- Fully autonomous software engineer
- Plans and executes complex software projects, bug fixes, app deployment, AI model training
- Strong SWE-bench performance

### Devika
- First open-source implementation of an Agentic Software Engineer
- Leverages Claude 3 for planning and coding
- Philosophy: "an intern you can offload work to, but not a replacement"

### OpenDevin
- Open-source replication of Devin
- Community-driven development and open standards
