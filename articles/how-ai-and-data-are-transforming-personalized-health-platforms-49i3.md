# How AI and Data are Transforming Personalized Health Platforms
**Author:** Seaflux Technologies  **Published:** December 22, 2025

## Overview
Describes a health technology platform shifting from generic wellness advice to personalized, data-driven recommendations. Integrates biomarker analysis with AI-powered recommendations to create individualized health strategies combining nutrition, fitness, and lifestyle interventions. Results: 98.4% satisfaction rate and 6% quarterly revenue growth.

## Key Concepts
- Problem: most health apps function as "glorified spreadsheets" lacking personalization based on individual biology
- Biomarker Integration: at-home blood testing provides biological data to inform recommendations
- Hybrid Model: AI triggers human health coach involvement when biomarkers trend negatively (86% engagement)
- Three-Pronged Strategy: metabolic meal plans, medically-informed fitness routines, lifestyle modifications
- Results: 98.4% satisfaction rate, 6% quarterly revenue growth
- Tech Stack: ReactJS, Next.js, Node.js on AWS Serverless, TypeScript, Zapier, Airtable
