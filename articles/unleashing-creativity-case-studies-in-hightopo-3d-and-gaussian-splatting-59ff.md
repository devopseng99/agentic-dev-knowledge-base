# Unleashing Creativity: Case Studies in Hightopo 3D and Gaussian Splatting
**Author:** Hightopo  **Published:** 2025-02-26

## Overview
3D Gaussian Splatting represents a breakthrough in 3D scene representation using deep learning technology with 3D Gaussian points instead of traditional triangle meshes. This article presents real case studies of Hightopo implementations.

## Key Concepts

### What is 3D Gaussian Splatting?
3D Gaussian Splatting uses millions of 3D Gaussian points (each with position, scale, rotation, and appearance attributes) rather than traditional triangle meshes, enabling high-quality real-time rendering.

### Basic Workflow
1. **Data Acquisition** — Smartphone video for small scenes; professional equipment (~30 minutes) for large scenes
2. **Model Training** — GPU computing processes and trains model data (~1 day depending on GPU capabilities)
3. **Rendering and Deployment** — Real-time rendering via Hightopo graphics engines

### Technical Advantages and Limitations

**Key Advantages:**
- High-quality rendering with excellent detail
- Efficient modeling with faster convergence than NeRF
- Strong adaptability across scenarios
- Real-time performance with smooth user experience
- Flexible data processing (images, videos, point clouds)

**Limitations:**
- High storage requirements affecting loading speed
- Compatibility challenges with triangle-based mesh models
- Demanding hardware requirements for large-scale rendering

### Case Study 1: Office Interior
Precise reconstruction of office interior using 3D Gaussian Splatting combined with Hightopo's graphics engine.

Features:
- **3D Navigation:** Interactive virtual avatars with employee information at workstations
- **Point Cloud Mode:** Dense point clusters representing office areas, meeting rooms, and facilities

### Case Study 2: Outdoor Street Scene
Zhongshan Road pedestrian street project in Xiamen capturing:
- Green glazed tile roofs
- Chinese cloud pattern decorations
- 1:1 scale recreation of buildings, vegetation, and street elements
- Virtual street tour for free environmental exploration
