# Top 10 Most-Starred Open-Source ERP and CRM on GitHub
**Author:** NocoBase  **Published:** May 6, 2025

## Overview
Ranks the most popular ERP and CRM projects on GitHub by star count. Modern systems increasingly integrate both functionalities — ERP solutions adding CRM capabilities and vice versa.

## Key Concepts

**The Top 10 Projects**

1. **Odoo (42.6k stars)** — Python/PostgreSQL; comprehensive modular architecture; Community Edition (LGPL v3) + Enterprise
2. **Twenty (27.7k stars)** — Node.js/React/PostgreSQL; modern CRM focused on simplicity; BSL-1.1 license
3. **ERPNext (24.7k stars)** — Frappe framework (Python, MariaDB, JavaScript/React); fully integrated ERP/CRM; GPL v3
4. **Monica (22.7k stars)** — PHP (Laravel)/MySQL/Vue.js; personal relationship management; AGPL v3
5. **Huly Platform (20.6k stars)** — Data sovereignty-focused, relation-driven architecture; Apache License 2.0
6. **NocoBase (15.5k stars)** — Node.js/React; no-code/low-code with plugin architecture; AGPL v3
7. **Krayin CRM (14.4k stars)** — Laravel; lightweight CRM for SMEs; MIT licensed
8. **Akaunting (8.6k stars)** — PHP (Laravel); accounting-focused ERP; multi-entity/multi-currency; GPL v3
9. **Grocy (7.5k stars)** — PHP; personal/household inventory and task management; MIT
10. **iDURAR ERP/CRM (7.3k stars)** — Node.js/React/MongoDB; integrated business operations for SMEs; MIT

**Selection Guide by Use Case**
- Enterprise deployment: ERPNext, iDURAR, Odoo Community
- Modern UI: Twenty, Krayin CRM
- Financial priority: Akaunting
- Compliance/sovereignty: Huly Platform
- High customization: NocoBase
