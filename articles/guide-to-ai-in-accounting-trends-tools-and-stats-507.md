# Guide to AI in Accounting: Trends, Tools, and Stats
**Author:** Integra Balance AI
**Published:** June 23, 2025

## Overview
Explores how AI is transforming accounting through automation, predictive analytics, and fraud detection. Notes that 80% of accounting work is likely to be automated in the next decade.

## Key Concepts
Key trends include AI-driven decision-making, real-time transaction analysis, and compliance automation. Popular tools highlighted include Xero, QuickBooks, and Expensify. AI supplements rather than replaces accountants.
