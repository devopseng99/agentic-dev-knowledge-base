**Author:** Furkan Gözükara  **Published:** July 29, 2024

## Overview
This guide introduces Kling AI, a video generation platform now accessible worldwide without cost. The article covers registration processes and explores core features including text-to-video, image-to-video, text-to-image, and image-to-image capabilities.

## Key Concepts
- Free daily "Inspiration Credits" (66 credits = ~6 videos per day)
- Subscription tiers for mainland China (Gold: ¥66, Platinum: ¥266, Diamond: ¥666)
- Enhanced foundation model with improved composition, color tone, and motion precision
- Maximum 10-second text-to-video generation duration
- Image-to-video animation features
- Comparison with OpenAI's SORA model
