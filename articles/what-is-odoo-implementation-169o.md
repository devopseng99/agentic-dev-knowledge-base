# What is Odoo Implementation?
**Author:** Bhavesh Gangani  **Published:** May 14, 2025

## Overview
Odoo is an open-source ERP platform enabling companies to manage various business functions within a single, integrated system. Implementation "goes far beyond just installing the software — it involves aligning the system with your business workflows."

## Key Concepts

**Core Modules**
- Sales/CRM
- Inventory
- Accounting
- HR
- Project Management
- Manufacturing

**7 Implementation Phases**
1. Requirement Gathering
2. System Design & Customization
3. Data Migration
4. Testing & Quality Assurance
5. Training
6. Go-Live
7. Post-Go-Live Support & Maintenance

**Implementation Team Options**
- Odoo Implementation Partners (certified experts)
- In-house teams (project managers, technical experts, business analysts, trainers)
- Hybrid approach combining both

**Timeline Estimates**
- Small business (1-3 modules): 4-8 weeks
- Medium business (4-8 modules): 2-4 months
- Enterprise (8+ modules): 4-6+ months

**Key Success Factors**
Strategic planning, stakeholder involvement, thorough testing, and comprehensive staff training to maximize ROI and operational efficiency.
