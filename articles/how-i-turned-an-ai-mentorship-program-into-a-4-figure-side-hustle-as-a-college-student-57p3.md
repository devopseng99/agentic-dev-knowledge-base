# How I Turned an AI Mentorship Program Into a 4-Figure Side Hustle (as a College Student)
**Author:** hda  **Published:** November 22, 2025

## Overview
A college student shares their experience completing Ritesh's Agent Rise mentorship program and documenting financial success and skill development in AI and software development over a four-month period.

## Key Concepts

- AI and agent development training through structured mentorship
- Building SaaS products from concept to launch
- Community collaboration among developers
- Monetization strategies for tech side projects
- Behavioral AI applications

**Project in Development**
A SaaS tool targeting doomscrolling prevention using AI-powered content detection, real-time user monitoring, and behavioral nudging to combat excessive social media usage.

**Financial Results**
The reviewer reports earning "over 4 figures" through part-time work while studying, with confidence in reaching five-figure earnings with continued effort.

**Key Takeaway**
AI agent development skills are monetizable even for students: start with freelance implementations, build toward productized services, leverage community resources for skill development.
