# 5 Groundbreaking AI Projects Using Blockchain
**Author:** Kevin
**Published:** March 18, 2025

## Overview
Discusses the intersection of AI and blockchain technology, covering five projects that combine AI's analytical capabilities with blockchain's secure, decentralized infrastructure.

## Key Concepts

### Five Featured Projects
1. **SingularityNET** - Open marketplace for creating, sharing, and monetizing AI models using the AGIX token
2. **OpenLedger** - Data blockchain supporting AI systems with focus on data integrity and payable AI models
3. **Ocean Protocol** - Blockchain-based data exchange enabling secure AI access to datasets while preserving privacy
4. **Fetch.AI** - Autonomous AI agents that interact with blockchain for supply chains and smart cities
5. **OpenAI's AI Governance** - Exploring blockchain for transparent audit trails and ethical AI oversight

### Benefits of Integration
- Data transparency
- Security and privacy protection
- Decentralized decision-making
- AI-driven automation for smart contracts
- AI-powered security in blockchain networks
