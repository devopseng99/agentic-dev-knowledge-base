## Overview

2. kube-copilot - Debugger and code generator (feiskyer/kube-copilot)
3. kubectl-ai - Natural language to kubectl (GoogleCloudPlatform/kubectl-ai)
4. KRR - Resource recommender for cost reduction (robusta-dev/krr)
