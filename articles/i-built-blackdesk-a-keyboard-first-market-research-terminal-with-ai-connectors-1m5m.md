# Blackdesk: Keyboard-First Market Research Terminal
**Author:** Florin Dumitru
**Published:** April 9, 2026

## Overview
Open-source terminal-based market research tool in Go with AI connectors, consolidating quotes, charts, news, screeners.

## Key Concepts

```bash
curl -fsSL https://blackdesk.ai/install | bash
blackdesk
```

Built with Go, Bubble Tea TUI, ports-and-adapters architecture. Yahoo Finance primary data adapter. Local-first operation with modular AI connectors.
