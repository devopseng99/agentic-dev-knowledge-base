# echo3D Integrates Support for Niantic's SPZ Format, Streamlining 3D Gaussian Splat Workflows
**Author:** echo3D  **Published:** 2026-01-04

## Overview
echo3D announced full platform support for the .SPZ file format, developed by Niantic Spatial. This integration addresses the challenge of managing large 3D Gaussian Splat files by providing compression technology for photorealistic 3D capture workflows.

## Key Concepts

### Problem Statement
The standard .PLY format for 3D Gaussian splats creates massive file sizes that are difficult to share and stream. SPZ was developed as a compressed alternative optimized specifically for this use case.

### Key Benefits of SPZ on echo3D
- **Massive Compression:** SPZ files are typically 10x smaller than their .PLY equivalents
- **High Fidelity:** Visual quality remains virtually unchanged despite file size reduction
- **Automated Conversion:** Users can upload standard .PLY files for automatic SPZ conversion
- **Web-Based Viewing:** Direct browser-based viewing within echo3D's console

### Quote from Leadership
Ben Pedazur, CTO at echo3D: "By integrating Niantic's SPZ format, we are removing the friction associated with heavy 3D Gaussian Splat files."

### About echo3D
echo3D is a 3D digital asset management (DAM) platform for teams to store, secure, optimize, and share 3D models and scans.
