# 10 Best Customer Service Chatbots for Your Business in 2025

**Author:** Anna Zilfugharyan
**Published:** March 21, 2025

## Overview

A comprehensive comparison of 10 customer service chatbot platforms, evaluating features, pricing, and limitations for business use cases.

## Key Concepts

### Chatbot Categories

- **Business chatbots:** Handle customer interactions, boost sales, and keep things flowing
- **Automation chatbots:** Manage repetitive tasks like scheduling
- **Personal chatbots:** Assist with reminders and daily tasks
- **Customer service chatbots:** Provide instant support, reducing wait times

### Top 10 Platforms

1. **Intercom** ($39-$139/seat/month) - AI-first platform with AI Agent, AI Copilot, and AI Analyst components
2. **Hoory AI** ($6-$42/agent/month) - All-in-one automation with trainable AI assistants and voice-enabled call centers
3. **Zendesk** ($55-$115/agent/month) - AI agents pre-trained on extensive datasets with customizable personas
4. **ProProfs Chat** ($19.99/operator/month) - Real-time assistance with customizable dashboards
5. **Dixa** ($49-$215/agent/month) - Mim OpenAI-powered chatbot for retail, finance, SaaS, travel
6. **Tidio** ($29-$2999/agent/month) - 24/7 availability with multichannel communication
7. **Freshchat** ($23-$95/agent/month) - Freddy AI-powered chatbots using NLP
8. **Chatfuel** ($23.99-$300/month) - No-code platform for Facebook Messenger and WhatsApp
9. **Meya** ($99-$3000/month) - Grid (backend coding), Orb (chatbot), Console (design/management)
10. **Kommunicate** ($100-$200/month) - Multi-channel support with bot-to-human handover

### Selection Criteria

Evaluate: setup complexity, integration needs, response mechanisms (AI-powered vs. rule-based), pricing model, and scalability requirements.
