# Community Spotlight: Building a Real-World ERP with NocoBase
**Author:** NocoBase  **Published:** December 4, 2025

## Overview
A Mexican entrepreneur spent over a decade attempting to build an integrated business system for his family's agricultural company before discovering NocoBase. After paying 60% upfront (~$6,600 USD) to developers who failed to complete the project, he tried Excel, Access databases, and 10+ no-code platforms before finding success.

## Key Concepts

**The Challenge**
Needed to cover: procurement, sales, inventory, logistics, projects, finance, HR, equipment maintenance, and fuel management. Previous attempts with Airtable, NocoDB failed due to per-user pricing, record caps, and insufficient features.

**Why NocoBase Succeeded**
- Free community version with no per-user charges
- Self-hosted on personal PC (8th-gen i3, 16GB RAM, 256GB SSD) costing approximately $129
- No user, record, or automation limitations
- Drag-and-drop interface requiring no coding knowledge
- Full customization for complex business needs

**Implementation Success**
System enables:
- Real-time field data capture
- Sophisticated analytics for supplier evaluation
- Customer segmentation
- Inventory optimization
- Workforce productivity measurement
Running continuously for over six months.

**Key Concepts Used**
- Database architecture and entity-relationship diagrams
- Role-based permissions and access control
- Workflow automation without code
- NocoBase 2.0 Code Agent capabilities

**Key Lesson:** Enterprise-grade ERP functionality is achievable for small businesses without enterprise budgets.
