# Simple Dockerfile for RealSense + ROS 2 Jazzy bring-up
**Author:** Md Sajib Pramanic  **Published:** May 4, 2026

## Overview
The article introduces a Docker-based solution for setting up Intel RealSense cameras with ROS 2. The author addresses the common challenge where "it works on one system, then breaks on another," offering a containerized approach to eliminate environmental inconsistencies.

## Key Concepts
- Docker containerization for ROS 2 robotics projects
- RealSense camera integration with ROS 2 Jazzy
- Environment standardization across development machines
- Reduced setup complexity for robotics developers
- Single Dockerfile eliminates dependency hell
