# Building a Full Player Onboarding + Gameplay Loop in UE5 (Dev Log #11)
**Author:** DomTech Gaming  **Published:** April 30, 2026

## Overview
Development log documenting the transition from isolated systems to an interconnected gameplay experience in Magickness, a discovery-driven MMORPG in Unreal Engine 5.4. Tests a complete player journey from account creation through early progression, with Enemy AI foundation included.

## Key Features Implemented
- Full onboarding sequence: registration → email verification → character creation
- Progression mechanics: Level 1 to Level 2 advancement
- Unlockable ability integration into active gameplay
- HUD hotbar and ability UI systems
- **Enemy AI Foundation**: Initial enemy AI systems for combat encounters
- Character statistics and progression tracking
- Morality system integrated into combat mechanics
- Inventory connected to player state management

## Key Concepts
- **Systems Integration Testing**: Where most game projects break — individual components that work in isolation fail when connected
- **Enemy AI Architecture**: Foundation layer for NPC combat behavior before adding complexity
- **Morality System**: Design philosophy of tracking player choices affecting game world
- **Discovery-Driven Design**: Abilities unlocked through gameplay rather than menus
- **AI-Assisted Development**: Claude used throughout development for architecture reviews and debugging
