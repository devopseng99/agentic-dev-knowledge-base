# AWS 2025: A Year of Agentic AI, Custom Chips, and Multicloud Bridges
**Author:** Damien Gallagher
**Published:** January 2, 2026

## Overview
Comprehensive review of AWS 2025 announcements across three themes: agentic AI everywhere, custom silicon at scale (Graviton5, Trainium3), and multicloud becoming real (AWS-Google partnership via AWS Interconnect). Covers Lambda Durable Functions, Bedrock AgentCore, Nova 2 models, and the surprising multicloud bridge.

## Key Concepts

Lambda Durable Functions enables building applications coordinating multiple steps from seconds to one year without paying for idle compute. Before this, you needed Step Functions or custom orchestration.

AWS Interconnect with Google Cloud: managed, high-speed private connections with quad-redundancy and MACsec encryption. Azure support coming 2026. "Multicloud is becoming practical."

Bedrock AgentCore: infrastructure for building, deploying, and operating AI agents at scale. Components include Runtime (session isolation, bidirectional streaming), Memory (episodic memory), Gateway (MCP-compatible tools), Identity (OAuth), Observability, Policy (Cedar), and Evaluations (13 built-in evaluators). SDK downloaded 2M+ times in 5 months.

AI agents are the new compute primitive -- from servers to containers to functions to agents.
