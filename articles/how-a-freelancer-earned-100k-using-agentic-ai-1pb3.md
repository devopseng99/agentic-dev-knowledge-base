# How a Freelancer Earned $100K Using Agentic AI
**Author:** Agami Technologies  **Published:** July 29, 2025

## Overview
The article profiles a freelancer who generated over $100,000 in six months by building autonomous AI agents for clients. It explores why agentic AI work is becoming lucrative and offers practical guidance for others entering this space.

## Key Concepts

- **Autonomous agents** that build full-stack applications and automate workflows
- Revenue models combining setup fees and monthly retainers
- Market demand from businesses lacking internal AI implementation expertise
- The execution gap between AI enthusiasm and actual deployment
- Risk mitigation through contracts and milestone-based payments

**Practical Recommendations**
- Start with specific business problems (lead generation, scheduling)
- Build credibility through pilot projects
- Diversify revenue through fixed packages, retainers, and custom work
- Price based on outcome value rather than hours spent

**Cautionary Tale**
One freelancer completed a $5,800 custom AI build only to have the client disappear. Always use contracts and collect upfront partial payments.
