# Workspaces: Building a Headless Company OS for API-first Processes and AI Agent Workflows
**Author:** Volker Schukai  **Published:** May 4, 2026

## Overview
Business software designed around APIs rather than UIs as the primary interface. Workspaces is an API-first, headless-capable business platform combining ERP core, CRM, commerce, PIM, documents, workflows, tasks, compliance, reporting, communication, and additional functions in one shared multi-tenant system.

## Key Concepts

**Core Platform Components**
- CRM
- ERP (core)
- Commerce (PIM)
- Document Management
- Workflows and Tasks
- Compliance and Reporting
- Chat and Newsletter functionality

**Headless-First Architecture**
Business software designed around APIs rather than UIs as the primary interface.

**API-First Design**
APIs expose business capabilities and processes, not just database objects. Clients can query available capabilities at runtime rather than hardcoding assumptions.

**Machine-Readable Contracts**
OpenAPI-like metadata with field-level descriptions for access control and privacy. Enables both humans and AI agents to discover and interact with business functionality.

**Multi-Tenant Operation**
Headless authentication, API keys, and permission-aware context management across organizations.

**AI Agent Readiness**
Systems designed for safe autonomous operation within business contexts:
- Stable API contracts enabling agent discovery
- Permission-aware context management
- Workflow integration through process orchestration
- Human oversight mechanisms built into architecture

**Key Design Journey**
From problems with traditional UI-first business software → API-first architectural solutions → future agent-based workflows. "An ongoing journey rather than a finished product."
