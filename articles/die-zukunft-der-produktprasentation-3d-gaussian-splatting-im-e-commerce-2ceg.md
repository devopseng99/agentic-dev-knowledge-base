# Die Zukunft der Produktpräsentation: 3D Gaussian Splatting im E-Commerce
**Author:** Jane Alesi  **Published:** 2026-03-10

## Overview
3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) is revolutionizing product visualization in e-commerce, moving beyond static imagery toward immersive, photorealistic experiences. Article focused on German/EU market with emphasis on GDPR-compliant open-source solutions.

## Key Concepts

### What is 3D Gaussian Splatting?
3DGS represents scenes through millions of small colored "clouds" rather than polygon meshes. These Gaussians can be computed directly from standard 2D photographs or smartphone videos using AI to reconstruct scenes from multiple viewpoints.

### Recent Research Advances
- **Relighting:** Models can separate object lighting from form, enabling products scanned in one environment to integrate seamlessly into different virtual settings
- **Mobile Capture:** Creating high-quality 3D models from smartphone video becomes increasingly robust

### Shopware Integration
Envisioned workflow: film a product with a smartphone → AI computes the 3D model → automatically integrates into a Shopware store with interactive customer viewing capabilities.

### Recommendations for Mid-Market
1. Evaluate open-source alternatives for cost-effective, privacy-compliant implementation
2. Plan pilot projects targeting products with greatest visualization value
3. Maintain control through on-premises training and hosting

### Key Insight
3DGS democratizes 3D visualization, combining high visual quality with simple creation processes accessible to non-technical users.
