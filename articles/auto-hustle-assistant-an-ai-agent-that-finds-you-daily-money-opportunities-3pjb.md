# Auto Hustle Assistant – An AI Agent That Finds You Daily Money Opportunities
**Author:** Tim Clever  **Published:** July 5, 2025

## Overview
An automated AI agent designed to identify daily income-generating opportunities. The system conducts morning web searches and delivers curated findings via email or chat.

## Key Concepts

- **Automated opportunity discovery** across multiple categories
- **Web scraping and filtering** using AI to identify relevant prospects
- **Daily reporting mechanism** summarizing 3–5 top opportunities
- **Multi-source aggregation** from specialized platforms and social media

**Opportunities Targeted**
- Remote employment positions
- International and African grant programs
- Cash competitions and contests
- Emerging digital product trends

**Information Sources**
- OpportunityDesk.org
- Remotive.io
- Twitter (keywords: "grant opportunity", "win $200")

**Workflow**
1. Searches designated platforms daily
2. Applies AI-driven filtering
3. Generates structured summaries (title, link, deadline, overview)
4. Distributes consolidated report

Available via Runner H platform.
