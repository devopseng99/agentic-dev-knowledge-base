# Automating Quality Control Queries with AI Assistance
**Author:** gabrielmateo
**Published:** 2025

## Overview
AI copilots serve as intelligent query assistants for quality control teams. Instead of manually searching through records, quality engineers can ask the AI questions in plain language to get instant answers about quality metrics and defect patterns.

## Key Concepts
Natural language interfaces for quality data querying, enabling non-technical QC teams to access complex analytics without SQL or specialized tools.
