# AI and Edge Computing: Enabling Real-Time Intelligence at Scale
**Author:** Vishal Uttam Mane  **Published:** 2026-04-15

## Overview
Examines how AI and edge computing work together to enable real-time intelligence in distributed systems at scale. Covers the architectural principles, use cases, and implementation challenges of deploying AI inference closer to data sources.

## Key Concepts
- Edge computing fundamentals: processing data at or near the source
- Latency reduction through local inference
- Bandwidth efficiency: transmitting only processed results
- AI model deployment strategies for constrained hardware
- Federated learning at the edge
- Hardware accelerators: NPUs, GPUs, FPGAs for edge AI
- Use cases: autonomous vehicles, industrial IoT, smart cameras, medical devices
- Security and privacy advantages of local processing
- Orchestration of edge AI deployments at scale
- Trade-offs: accuracy vs latency vs power vs cost
