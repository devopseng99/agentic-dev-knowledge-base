# Prompt Like a Pro: Zero-Shot, Few-Shot & Chain-of-Thought Explained Simply

**Author:** Muskan Fatima
**Published:** June 14, 2025

## Overview
Explains three fundamental prompting techniques: zero-shot (direct questions without examples), few-shot (1-3 sample demonstrations), and chain-of-thought (step-by-step reasoning with 5-9+ examples). CoT excels for complex reasoning, coding, and multi-step tasks.

## Key Concepts
- **Zero-Shot:** Direct questions relying entirely on existing model knowledge
- **Few-Shot:** 1-3 examples demonstrating desired design or format
- **Chain-of-Thought:** Requires the AI to reason step by step with explicit logical problem-solving before generating answers
