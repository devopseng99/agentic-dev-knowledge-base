# How AI is Revolutionizing Renewable Energy Grids
**Author:** Suraj
**Published:** April 27, 2025

## Overview
Explores how AI transforms renewable energy infrastructure through predictive maintenance, efficiency optimization, and grid management. The renewable energy market is projected to grow from $1.34 trillion in 2024 to $5.62 trillion by 2033.

## Key Concepts
Includes a Python code example for energy output prediction. Covers real-world applications from Siemens Energy, Google DeepMind, and Tesla. Encourages developers to contribute using TensorFlow, PyTorch, and weather/energy APIs.
