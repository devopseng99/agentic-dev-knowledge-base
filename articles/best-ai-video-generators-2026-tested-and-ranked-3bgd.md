### Tool Analysis

**HeyGen (Top Pick)**
Transforms production economics: "Write a script, select or create an avatar, generate the video. From script approval to finished video is about 20 minutes." The multilingual dubbing feature with synchronized lip movements provides significant value for international marketing.

**Synthesia (Enterprise Leader)**
Purpose-built for corporate L&D with mature enterprise features including SSO, user permissions, and LMS integrations. Best for organizations scaling training content.

**Descript (Editor's Choice)**
Text-based editing paradigm: "Delete a word in the transcript, the corresponding video clip disappears." Combines transcription, audio overdub, and filler word removal for podcast and talking-head content.

**Runway (Creative Focus)**
Gen-3 Alpha model produces cinematic-quality text-to-video output handling "lighting, depth of field, and motion physics." Broader creative platform with green screen removal and motion capture.

**Pictory & Lumen5**
Narrow-use tools for blog-to-video repurposing with stock footage automation. Lumen5 emphasizes template design and brand customization for social media.

### Buyer Decision Framework
- **Spokesperson content:** HeyGen or Synthesia
- **Recording/editing:** Descript
- **Cinematic creation:** Runway
- **Content repurposing:** Pictory or Lumen5
- **Budget-conscious volume:** Kling AI (free tier available)
