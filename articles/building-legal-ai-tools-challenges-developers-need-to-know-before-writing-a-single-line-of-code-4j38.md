# Building Legal AI Tools: Challenges Developers Need to Know Before Writing a Single Line of Code
**Author:** Evdokia Pappa  **Published:** May 10, 2025

## Overview
International lawyer and founding partner of Evdokia Pappa Law Firm shares critical considerations for developers entering the legal technology space. "Legal professionals need better, faster tools," but builders must navigate "real-world landmines."

## Key Concepts

**5 Key Challenges**

1. **Data Sensitivity & Confidentiality**
   Legal information often involves attorney-client privilege and sealed documents. Unsafe scraping, storage, or training data practices expose developers and users to liability.

2. **Liability & Explainability Requirements**
   When AI generates compliance outputs (GDPR audits, AML flags), the tool influences legal responsibility. "Many jurisdictions require explainability, auditability, and even human-in-the-loop systems."

3. **Language Complexity**
   "Legal language is dense, full of nuance, and jurisdiction-dependent." Identical phrases may carry opposite meanings contextually.

4. **Existing Solutions**
   "Don't reinvent the wheel" — established APIs and frameworks already serve legal applications.

5. **User Trust & UX Design**
   "Legal professionals are risk-averse by design. They value clarity, documentation, and accuracy over speed." Interface design must reflect these priorities to ensure adoption.

**Key Design Principles for Legal AI**
- Attorney-client privilege protection
- Explainable and auditable outputs
- Human-in-the-loop for consequential decisions
- Clear disclaimers about the limits of AI legal advice
- Jurisdiction-aware responses
