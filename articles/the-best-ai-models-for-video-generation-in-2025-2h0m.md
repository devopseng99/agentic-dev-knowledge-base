# The Best AI Models for Video Generation in 2025
**Author:** Andrew Baisden  **Published:** January 14, 2025

## Overview
The article surveys 12 prominent AI video generation models available in 2025. It highlights how advances in generative AI have made these tools considerably simple to create professional-quality content for creators across industries.

## Key Concepts
- Text-to-video generation capabilities
- Accessibility of professional-grade tools
- Both commercial and open-source models

## AI Models Featured

1. **OpenAI Sora** (December 2024) - Text-to-video generation
2. **Kling AI** (June 2024) - Kuaishou AI Team platform
3. **Hailuo AI** (September 2024) - MiniMax-developed tool
4. **Pika AI** (November 2023) - Text/image prompt editing
5. **Mochi 1** (October 2024) - Open-source Genmo AI model
6. **Hunyuan Video** (December 2024) - Tencent platform
7. **Runway** (February 2023) - Runway AI platform
8. **Luma Labs Dream Machine** (June 2024) - Photorealistic generation
9. **Haiper AI** (October 2024) - London-based startup
10. **CogVideo** (May 2022) - Tsinghua University open-source
11. **Pyramidal Flow** (October 2024) - Academic collaboration
12. **LTX Studio** (March 2024) - Lightricks filmmaking platform
