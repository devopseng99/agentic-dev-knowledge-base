# AI in Auditing and Accounting: Innovation or Impossibility?
**Author:** EQCPA
**Published:** May 4, 2025

## Overview
Explores AI's dual role in accounting and auditing. While AI excels at automating repetitive tasks like fraud detection and data analysis, professional judgment, ethical reasoning, and contextual understanding remain beyond current AI capabilities.

## Key Concepts
Regulatory frameworks built around human accountability present additional barriers to full automation. A collaborative model where AI handles data-intensive work while humans focus on interpretation and advisory services represents the most realistic future.
