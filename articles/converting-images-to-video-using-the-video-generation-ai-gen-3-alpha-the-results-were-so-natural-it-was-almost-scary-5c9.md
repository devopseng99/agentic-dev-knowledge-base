# Converting Images to Video Using the Video Generation AI "Gen-3 Alpha"
**Author:** nabata  **Published:** September 29, 2024

## Overview
The article explores Runway's Gen-3 Alpha video generation tool, demonstrating how static images can be converted into realistic videos through AI. The author compares results with Dream Machine and evaluates the quality of motion generation.

## Key Concepts

- **Gen-3 Alpha**: Runway's video generation AI requiring paid subscription (Standard plan: $15/month)
- **Credit System**: Standard plan provides 625 credits monthly; Gen-3 Alpha consumes 10 credits per video second
- **Image Requirements**: Videos require 1280x768 resolution input
- **Prompt-Based Generation**: Natural language prompts guide motion and behavior
- **Comparison Analysis**: Dream Machine produces noticeably more distortion, particularly around facial features
