# Building a Free AI Mental Health Chat for Privacy and Support
**Author:** LOLkkk  **Published:** September 13, 2025

## Overview
Presents Mental AI Answer, a free web application designed to provide accessible mental health support through an AI chatbot interface. Addresses barriers to therapy access by offering anonymous, judgment-free conversation support available around the clock.

## Key Concepts
- Privacy-First Design: 100% anonymous, no login required for users
- Accessibility: available 24/7 without cost barriers
- Support Areas: anxiety, stress management, and self-reflection guidance
- Deployment: Vercel-hosted web application
