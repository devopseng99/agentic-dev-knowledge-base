# Hackathon scanner to spark your next big idea!
**Author:** Julian
**Published:** July 7, 2025

## Overview
A hackathon scanner tool for DoraHacks that aggregates winning project data from multiple hackathon report pages into organized Google Sheets. Built for the Runner H AI Agent Prompting Challenge.

## Key Concepts
- Data aggregation from DoraHacks hackathon reports (XION, Calimero x ICP, Cambrian Hack, AIXFinTech, Core Gaming)
- Structured organization across three sheets (Hackathon Details, Winners, Winner Projects)
- Systematic web scraping with consistent field labeling
- Developer inspiration and investor talent identification
