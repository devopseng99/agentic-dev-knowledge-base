# How a Solo Founder Runs a Business with 20+ AI Agents
**Author:** Mupengyi  **Published:** February 17, 2026

## Overview
A solo entrepreneur describes operating a bootstrapped tech company using 20+ AI agents instead of traditional employees. The founder manages real-world automation across Instagram customer service, political social media monitoring, and venture capital outreach using custom agent infrastructure.

## Key Concepts

- Agent-based business operations replacing traditional staffing
- Persistent, memory-equipped AI agents with web browsing and code execution capabilities
- Multi-agent coordination through protocols and heartbeat systems
- Parallel sub-agent deployment for scalable task handling
- Shift from SaaS to "AIaaS" business models
- **OpenClaw** – Open-source AI agent runtime: github.com/openclaw/openclaw
- **mupengism** – Custom agent-to-agent communication protocol: npmjs.com/package/mupengism

**Real-World Use Cases:**
1. Instagram DM auto-responses with 90% automation
2. Real-time political social media monitoring dashboard
3. Personalized venture capital cold outreach (15% reply rate)
