# SupplyGuard AI: Real-Time Supply Chain Risk Detection Agent
**Author:** Aniruddha Adak
**Published:** May 12, 2025

## Overview
An intelligent monitoring system leveraging Bright Data to detect supply chain disruptions in real-time by scanning news sources, social media, government announcements, and transportation networks. Achieves 73% faster response to disruptions and reduces false positives from 40% to under 8%.

## Key Concepts
Features include global event monitoring, supplier health tracking, transportation network monitoring, geopolitical risk assessment, and environmental event detection. Performance metrics show 9.3 additional days of response time and cost reductions in expedited shipping (56%) and production stoppages (61%).
