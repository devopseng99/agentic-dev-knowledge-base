# From Broken Onboarding to a Playable Loop — Fixing Player Flow, Combat, and HUD in UE5
**Author:** DomTech Gaming  **Published:** April 13, 2026

## Overview
Dev Log #6 documents the transition of Magickness from isolated systems to an integrated, playable gameplay loop. Connects account creation, email verification, and character creation into a cohesive player experience. "This is where most systems actually break — at integration."

## Key Concepts
- **System Integration as Critical Milestone**: Individual systems that pass unit testing often fail when connected — integration reveals hidden dependencies and state management issues
- **Player Flow Stabilization**: Onboarding → gameplay path made reliable and predictable
- **Locomotion and Combat Blending**: Systems that worked independently must blend seamlessly in active gameplay
- **HUD Functionality**: Stamina mechanics and ability indicators integrated into active HUD
- **Discovery-Driven MMORPG Philosophy**: Systems must be reliable for meaningful player experimentation

## Tech Stack
- Unreal Engine 5.4
- Mixamo (animation)
- MakeHuman (character meshes)
- Custom UI and gameplay systems
