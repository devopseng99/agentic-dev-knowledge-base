# Building a One-Person Company Powered by AI
**Author:** Jaejin Song  **Published:** March 18, 2026

## Overview
A solo founder reflects on six months building independent products while leveraging AI to overcome time constraints. After shipping two products with zero revenue, the author discovered that treating AI as an "employee" rather than a tool dramatically improved efficiency.

## Key Concepts

- Solo entrepreneurship bottlenecks (planning, development, design, marketing, debugging)
- AI delegation model: founder sets direction, AI executes, founder reviews
- Claude Max as productivity accelerator
- Speed metrics: 2 products in 6 months → targeting 1 per month
- Product portfolio:
  - Vybbi (YouTube/Instagram spam detection)
  - KWatch (Korean stock data)
  - Third product in development

**Key Quote**
"A CEO doesn't tell an engineer which lines of code to write. They say 'build this feature,' the engineer figures out how to do it — that's how I use AI now."
