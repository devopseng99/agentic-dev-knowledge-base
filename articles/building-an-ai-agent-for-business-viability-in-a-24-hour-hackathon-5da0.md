# Building an AI Agent for Business Viability In a 24 Hour Hackathon
**Author:** Favor Charles Owuor
**Published:** February 5, 2026

## Overview
A five-person team built Microbiz, a business viability checker in a 24-hour hackathon with a core constraint: AI could not be the product itself but had to support a real system. The tool validates business concepts using quantitative financial analysis.

## Key Concepts
- Django backend with deterministic financial calculations
- Groq AI API for higher-level reasoning about market conditions and pricing realism
- AI agents amplify systems rather than replace solid foundations
- Prompt engineering for controlled AI behavior
- "Garbage In Garbage Out" principles apply to AI-powered financial analysis

The system requires structured inputs (starting capital, pricing strategy, expected monthly sales, operating costs, time horizon) and computes revenue, total costs, profit margins, and sustainability indicators before passing to AI for viability recommendations.
