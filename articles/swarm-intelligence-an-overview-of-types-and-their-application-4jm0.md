# Swarm Intelligence: An overview of some types and their application
**Author:** Raf Lefever  **Published:** July 24, 2024 (Edited August 21, 2024)

## Overview
Introductory article exploring Swarm Intelligence (SI), an AI field inspired by collective behavior in nature. Introduces six major SI methodologies with real-world applications spanning robotics, optimization, and logistics. Marks the beginning of a series promising deeper technical details.

## Key Concepts
1. **Ant Colony Optimization (ACO)** — Uses pheromone trails for pathfinding; applied to traveling salesman and vehicle routing problems
2. **Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO)** — Models bird flocking behavior; used in neural network training and financial modeling
3. **Bee Colony Optimization (BCO)** — Mimics honey bee foraging; useful for clustering and telecommunications optimization
4. **Firefly Algorithm** — Based on firefly attraction; employed in aerodynamic design
5. **Artificial Immune Systems (AIS)** — Replicates immune defense; applied to cybersecurity and intrusion detection
6. **Bacterial Foraging Optimization (BFO)** — Models bacterial movement; used in environmental monitoring
