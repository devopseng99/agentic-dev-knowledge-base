# The Real Cost of Running an AI Agent to Make Money Online
**Author:** Hopkins Jesse  **Published:** April 2, 2026

## Overview
A 30-day experiment running an autonomous AI agent to pursue online income through bounty hunting, content creation, and freelance bidding. Despite the hype, the experiment yielded zero revenue while incurring substantial hidden costs.

## Key Concepts

1. **API Costs** - Daily expenses ranged from $1.14-$5.50, totaling $87.40 monthly
2. **Time Investment** - 45 hours of human oversight required despite automation
3. **Opportunity Costs** - Foregone income from traditional freelancing (~$30-50/hour)
4. **Bounty Hunting Results** - 23 opportunities identified, 3 PRs submitted, 0 payments received
5. **AI Agent Capabilities** - Strengths in research/filtering; limitations in account management and negotiation
6. **ROI Analysis** - Net loss of $987.40 when excluding labor savings calculations

**Key Finding**
"AI agents in 2026 are excellent research assistants but terrible money-makers" — capable of research and drafting yet unable to handle authentication or client negotiation.
