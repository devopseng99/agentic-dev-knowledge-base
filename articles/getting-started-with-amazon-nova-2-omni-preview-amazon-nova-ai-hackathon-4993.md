# Getting started with Amazon Nova 2 Omni (Preview) & Amazon Nova AI hackathon
**Author:** Wendy Wong
**Published:** February 20, 2026

## Overview
Introduction to the Amazon Nova 2 family of foundation models and the Amazon Nova AI Hackathon (Feb 2 - Mar 16, 2026). Covers Nova 2 Lite, Pro, Sonic, and Omni models with a 1-million-token context window.

## Key Concepts
- **Nova 2 Lite** - Cost-effective multimodal reasoning for fast text/code generation
- **Nova 2 Pro** - Extended thinking with multimodal reasoning
- **Nova 2 Sonic** - Speech-to-speech for conversational AI and contact centers
- **Nova 2 Omni** - One-size-fits-all multimodal model handling text, images, video, speech (200 languages text, 10 languages speech)
- Innovation categories: Agentic AI, Multimodal understanding, UI automation, Voice experiences
- $200 AWS credits prize for blog submissions
