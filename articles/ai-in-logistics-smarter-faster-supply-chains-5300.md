# AI in Logistics: Smarter, Faster Supply Chains
**Author:** Bitontree
**Published:** April 24, 2025

## Overview
Explores how AI is revolutionizing logistics and supply chain management through automated warehousing, fleet management optimization, and predictive analytics. The global AI logistics market is projected to grow from $12 billion in 2023 to $549 billion by 2033.

## Key Concepts
Companies like Logiwa, FourKites, and Coyote Logistics lead implementation through machine learning and real-time data analysis technologies for demand forecasting, route optimization, and warehouse automation.
