# How to use Google Gemini AI for Agriculture Productivity
**Author:** vishalmysore
**Published:** May 7, 2024

## Overview
Demonstrates practical applications of Google's Gemini AI for crop disease and pest identification using Tools4AI, a Java-based framework. Shows image-to-structured-data conversion for real-time analysis, including weather assessment, crop recommendations, and locust control.
