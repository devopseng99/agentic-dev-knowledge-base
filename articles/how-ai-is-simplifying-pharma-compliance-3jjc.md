# How AI is Simplifying Pharma Compliance
**Author:** Xccelerance Technologies  **Published:** September 19, 2025

## Overview
Examines how Agentic AI is transforming pharmaceutical regulatory compliance. Autonomous AI systems can streamline the traditionally manual, time-consuming process of preparing drug submissions for regulatory agencies like the FDA and EMA.

## Key Concepts
- Agentic AI as Digital Assistant: AI systems operating autonomously to manage regulatory submission tasks
- Data Integration: collecting information from lab systems, clinical trial platforms, and manufacturing tools
- Document Preparation: automated writing and structuring of regulatory documents with consistent formatting
- Compliance Verification: cross-checking submissions against regulatory guidelines to identify gaps
- Time and Error Reduction: accelerating approval timelines while minimizing documentation mistakes
- Human-AI Collaboration: experts focusing on strategy while AI handles repetitive work
