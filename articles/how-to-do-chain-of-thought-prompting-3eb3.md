# How to do Chain of Thought Prompting?

**Author:** Nik L.
**Published:** November 21, 2024

## Overview
Introduces CoT prompting using an analogy of mentoring a junior developer -- breaking down complex tasks into manageable steps rather than overwhelming the AI with entire specifications at once.

## Key Concepts - The Four-Part Framework

1. **Know Your Mission** - Establish crystal-clear, specific goals
2. **Break It Down** - Divide large tasks into smaller pieces
3. **Tell a Story** - Connect steps using transitional phrases like "next up," "after that," and "then we'll"
4. **Ask for the Why** - Request explanations for AI reasoning choices
