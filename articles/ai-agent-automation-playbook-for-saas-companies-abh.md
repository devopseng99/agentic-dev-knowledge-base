# AI Agent Automation Playbook for SaaS Companies
**Author:** Afzaal Muhammad  **Published:** May 7, 2026

## Overview
A phased approach to implementing AI agents in SaaS companies, emphasizing practical deployment over aspirational automation. Based on 109 business deployments across Australia, Canada, and the US.

## Key Concepts

### Initial Assessment Framework
Categorize tasks into four buckets based on repetitiveness, rule-based nature, volume, and risk level. Measure by "average minutes per task, multiplied by frequency per week" to identify genuine automation candidates.

### Week 1 Quick Wins
- Inbound lead qualification and routing
- Support ticket triage and tier-1 responses
- Meeting scheduling and preparation
- Daily revenue and pipeline reporting

### Month 1 Medium-Effort Automations
- Customer onboarding sequences
- Outbound prospecting drafts (human-reviewed before sending)
- Churn signal detection
- Invoice processing and AR follow-up

### Months 2-3 Advanced Workflows
- Multi-agent collaboration across sales, CS, and finance
- Product feedback loop automation
- Engineering on-call augmentation
- Competitive intelligence monitoring

### What Remains Manual
High-stakes decisions that should stay human-controlled: pricing negotiations, hiring decisions, customer terminations, crisis communications, strategic messaging, and performance reviews.

### Success Metrics
- Hours returned per role
- Time-to-first-response
- Agent escalation rates (targeting 15-25%)
- Cost per resolved task
- Customer-facing accuracy above 95%

### Pricing Context
Typical costs for a 40-person SaaS company running 5-8 agents: $2,500-$5,000/month. This compares favorably against the cost of additional headcount for the same tasks.
