# How AI Agents Are Revolutionising Web3 Automation
**Author:** Kevin
**Published:** July 25, 2025

## Overview
Explores how AI agents are emerging as a powerful trend in Web3, combining ChatGPT-style intelligence with smart contract wallet capabilities for autonomous on-chain actions.

## Key Concepts

### What AI Agents Do in Web3
- Monitor real-time market conditions
- Execute transactions based on predefined strategies
- Interact with smart contracts independently
- Optimize DeFi yield farming or NFT management without manual intervention

### Four Mechanisms of Improvement
1. **Learn and Adapt**: Analyze market data and transaction histories for smarter decisions
2. **Reduce Human Error**: Operate continuously without sleep constraints
3. **Enable Autonomous dApps**: AI agents managing liquidity pools, negotiating deals, handling DAO proposals
4. **Enhance Security**: Continuous monitoring for suspicious transactions and contract vulnerabilities

### Real-World Example
DeFi protocols experimenting with AI-powered portfolio managers that analyze yield rates across platforms and reallocate liquidity, maximizing returns while minimizing risk.
