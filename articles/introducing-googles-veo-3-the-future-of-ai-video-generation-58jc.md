# Introducing Google's Veo 3: The Future of AI Video Generation
**Author:** Stellan  **Published:** May 26, 2025

## Overview
The article introduces Google's Veo 3, an advanced AI video generation model unveiled at Google I/O 2025. It highlights the capability to generate professional-quality videos from text or image prompts with native audio synchronization.

## Key Concepts

1. **Native Audio Generation** - For the first time, the model generates videos with dialogue, sound effects, and synchronized background audio
2. **High-Quality Output** - Supports up to 4K resolution at 60fps for smooth, professional results
3. **Complex Prompt Understanding** - Handles detailed scene descriptions, character actions, and narrative sequences
4. **Reference Image Support** - Maintains consistency across characters, scenes, and visual styles
5. **Multi-Platform Access** - Available through Gemini app (Ultra subscribers), Vertex AI (enterprise), and Flow (Google's AI filmmaking tool)
6. **Third-Party Alternatives** - Platforms like veo3.art offering flexible pricing and additional features
