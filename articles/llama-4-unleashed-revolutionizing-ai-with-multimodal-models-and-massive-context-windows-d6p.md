# Llama 4 Unleashed: Revolutionizing AI with Multimodal Models and Massive Context Windows
**Author:** ANIRUDDHA ADAK  **Published:** April 5, 2025

## Overview
Meta's Llama 4 suite introduces two new open-weight AI models designed for efficiency and multimodal capabilities. The announcement emphasizes accessibility through open-source distribution while highlighting competitive performance against established models like GPT-4o and Claude Sonnet 3.7.

## Key Concepts

- **Llama 4 Scout**: 17 billion active parameters across 16 experts; supports a 10 million token context window; runs on single NVIDIA H100 GPU
- **Llama 4 Maverick**: 17 billion active parameters across 128 experts; native multimodal (text + image) capabilities; matches DeepSeek v3 performance
- **Llama 4 Behemoth**: 288 billion active parameters (still in training); serves as teacher model for Scout and Maverick distillation
- **Mixture-of-Experts (MoE)**: Architecture enabling selective parameter activation for efficiency
- **Training Scale**: Over 30 trillion tokens across text, images, and video
- **Safety Measures**: Llama Guard, Prompt Guard, red-teaming; refusal rate reduced to under 2%

Models available at llama.com and Hugging Face.
