# How IoT and AI Work Together in Healthcare
**Author:** Evelina Wright  **Published:** December 30, 2025

## Overview
Explains how Internet of Things (IoT) devices and Artificial Intelligence converge to transform healthcare delivery. "IoT collects real-time data, while AI turns that data into insights that support better care." The combination enables continuous patient monitoring, improves clinical decision-making, and enhances patient engagement across various care settings.

## Key Concepts
- IoT in Healthcare: connected devices (wearables, smart medical equipment, monitoring systems) that continuously collect patient data such as vital signs
- AI Applications: machine learning algorithms that analyze data patterns, support diagnosis, predict health risks, and enable clinical decision support
- Integration Benefits: remote patient monitoring, predictive health analytics, smart hospital asset tracking, real-time clinical alerts
- Challenges: data security concerns, system integration complexity, staff training requirements, and managing large data volumes
