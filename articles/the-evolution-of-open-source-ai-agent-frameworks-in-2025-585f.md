# The Evolution of Open-Source AI Agent Frameworks in 2025

**Author:** Seth Keddy
**Published:** September 18, 2025

## Overview
AI development has shifted from experimental chatbots to practical, production-grade autonomous systems. "2023-2024 was about experimentation, 2025 is about operationalization."

## Key Concepts

### Framework Landscape
- **LangChain + LangGraph:** Prototyping to structured workflows; less production-hardened without customization
- **CrewAI:** Human-like team collaboration; ideal for content pipelines, coordination challenges at scale
- **AutoGen:** Conversational multi-agent orchestration; latency concerns in high-throughput scenarios
- **Semantic Kernel:** Enterprise integration with C#/.NET/Python support; steep learning curve
- **ModelScope-Agent:** API-first approach with open-source LLMs; fewer guardrails

### Rising Players
- LlamaIndex: Evolving toward knowledge-rich agent orchestration
- Haystack 2.0: From document retrieval to multi-agent systems
- SkyPilot & Ray AIR: Distributed computing intersecting with agents
