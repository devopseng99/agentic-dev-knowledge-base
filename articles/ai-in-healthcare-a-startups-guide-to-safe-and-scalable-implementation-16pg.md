# AI in Healthcare - A Startup's Guide to Safe and Scalable Implementation
**Author:** Quokka Labs  **Published:** August 28, 2025

## Overview
Comprehensive guide addressing how healthcare startups can implement AI safely while maintaining scalability. Emphasizes that speed without safety creates risks—regulatory failures, patient data breaches, and loss of clinical trust. AI in healthcare projected to save around $150 billion each year in the U.S. by 2026, but only through responsible implementation.

## Key Concepts
- Patient Data Privacy as Foundation: de-identification, federated learning, minimal data collection, regular security audits
- Regulatory Compliance: HIPAA encryption, access controls, audit trails, and consent management
- Scalable Architecture: modular pipelines, cloud-ready infrastructure, MLOps practices, EHR interoperability
- Technology Selection: conversational AI for structured workflows; generative AI for unstructured tasks
- Adoption Metrics: measuring usage and workflow impact, not solely accuracy scores
- Real-World Lessons: focus on single use cases initially, staff training, incremental scaling
- AI projected to save $150 billion/year in U.S. healthcare by 2026
