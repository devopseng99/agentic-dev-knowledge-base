# How DRBench Stress-Tests AI Agents for Real-World Enterprise Research
**Author:** Max aka Mosheh  **Published:** October 18, 2025

## Overview
Introduces DRBench, a testing framework designed to validate AI agents in realistic business environments rather than controlled settings. Emphasizes the gap between theoretical performance and practical enterprise deployment.

## Key Concepts
1. **Real-World Testing Need** — Standard dashboards fail to reveal agent performance amid messy, unstructured data across files, emails, and chats
2. **Concrete Metrics** — Measures recall, accuracy, and report clarity while incorporating "decoys" to identify false leads
3. **Case Study Results** — A vendor research agent achieved: 43% faster search times, recall improvement from 62% to 88%, 51% reduction in false leads
4. **DRBench-Inspired Methodology:**
   - Define clear questions and time constraints
   - Build controlled ground-truth datasets
   - Introduce decoys and outdated sources
   - Score recall and factual accuracy
   - Require source citations

"You get immediate signal on gaps and risks. You fix prompts, tools, and data with proof, not vibes."
