# Improving Team Retrospectives with Kollabe's Template Generator
**Author:** Matt  **Published:** April 15, 2025

## Overview
The article discusses how team retrospectives can become monotonous and offers Kollabe's Retrospective Template Generator as a solution to inject variety and engagement into these agile ceremonies.

## Key Concepts
- **Retrospectives**: Team meetings for reflecting on work, identifying patterns, and making improvements
- **Template Generator**: A tool offering diverse formats beyond standard "What went well/What didn't" frameworks
- **Themed Retrospectives**: Different conceptual frames (e.g., "Retro Gaming," "Wild West") to maintain participant engagement
- **Variety in Formats**: Different question structures that prompt novel insights

The article covers how Kollabe provides themed retrospective templates that go beyond the standard "Start/Stop/Continue" or "What went well/What didn't" formats. The author is a Senior Software Engineer at SafetyCulture based in Sydney, Australia, and builds Kollabe as a side project.

Referenced: *Agile Retrospectives: Making Good Teams Great* by Esther Derby and Diana Larsen
