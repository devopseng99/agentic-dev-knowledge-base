# Empowering Contact Center Agents with Amazon Q Business
**Author:** Vini Ganancio
**Published:** May 28, 2025

## Overview
How Amazon Q Business transforms contact center operations by providing context-aware conversational support across connected systems (Confluence, SharePoint, Salesforce, S3). Covers agent workflow optimization, performance measurement, and continuous improvement strategies.

## Key Concepts

### Amazon Q Business for Contact Centers
- Retrieves answers from Confluence, internal documents, SharePoint, Salesforce, S3 buckets
- Agents ask natural questions like "What's the cancellation policy for enterprise customers?"
- Provides answers with source citations for verification
- Can trigger actions in Salesforce or Jira without switching applications
- Pairs with Amazon Connect as real-time assistant during calls

### Performance Metrics Impact
- Average handle time: 15-25% reduction
- First-contact resolution: 10-20% improvement
- Search time reduction: 50-70%
- Net Promoter Score: 5-15 point improvement

### Continuous Improvement Framework
1. Knowledge base enrichment with regular content review cycles
2. Agent training on effective query formulation
3. Performance measurement comparing pre/post implementation KPIs
